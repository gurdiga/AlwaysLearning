Hey folks! I am on shoe box swap group for homeschoolers. Members
swap shoe boxes with other families every month. The owner of the
group partners the members each month with a different family.
Families fill the shoe box with crafts and/or items from thier home
state or country. It's a great way to "meet" other homeschooling
familes from around the world. And, the more members we have, the
more swapping we can do! If you join now, you will be in time for
the November swap.. we have already paired up this month. CHECK IT
OUT!

Will and I drove about 70 miles one-way to go roller skating with another
homeschool group. We're expanding our circles, and one of the familes in that
group attends some of our unschooler things. Will hasn't been skating since
we left Memphis, so we thought it would be fun, and we planned to hit some
flea markets on the way home.

We didn't know we would have a once in a lifetime opportunity. We saw the
third largest meteorite ever found in the world today, live and in person, in
the back of a pick-up truck and learned all about it from its owner, a
homeschool dad from Huntsville, AR. It's worth more than a million dollars.

Here's the website for the meteorite: worldrecordmeteorite.com You should
be hearing more about it outside of Kansas and on the Discovery Channel and
other places soon. He's fielding lots and lots of calls and inquiries.

It was well worth the 140 miles!

Karen

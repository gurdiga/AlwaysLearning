The nightstand beside my bed is our family sock drawer. Brenna, Logan and I
all wear the same size...all white lowcut sport socks. Broc's are twice the
size of the rest. The socks just get thrown into the drawer and when anyone
needs a pair they rummage through and find a pair that may or may not match.
I like mine to match.

Every few months, Logan will sort the socks because it's something he likes
to do and we get rid of all the ones that no longer have matches (where do
they go??? <g>)

Right now, they are all matched up which I find is a sign of our typical
summers ...Not so many places we need to get to so we have time for doing a few
more jigsaw puzzles and organizing our sock drawer.

I'm proud to announce a NEW program for teenage homeschoolers & unschoolers: Homeschool Leadership Retreats.

Homeschool Leadership Retreats ("HLR") offers fun, independence-building sleep-away programs for self-directed teens ages 14-17. We give teens the tools, attitudes, and confidence to figure out and pursue their dreams.

Our first retreat will be held in Ashland, Oregon, from May 9-23, 2010. This two-week adventure includes exciting community-based activities, interactive leadership workshops, and special trips to Crater Lake, the Oregon Shakespeare Festival, and a high ropes course!

You can learn more about HLR and our upcoming retreats at:
http://www.homeschoolleadershipretreats.com

An $150 Early Enrollment discount is available until January 9th, and $200 work-trade scholarships are available as well.

Thanks for your time!

Blake Boles
(Co-founder of Unschool Adventures and author of College Without High School)

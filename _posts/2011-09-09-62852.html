I'm listening to Ronnie Maier's talk "Roadblocks and Roundabouts"

RIDE

After a story about how when driving in England, Ronnie figured out you can stay on the roundabout until you decide which exit to take, she came up with a little system for dealing with panic about which direction to take.

Recognize your triggers. Tension? Queasiness? What are your clues?

Identify the problem.

Decide what to do, or how to react, or how to respond.

Explore together. Get off the roundabout and get back on. Find alternatives.

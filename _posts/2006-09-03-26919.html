I remember seeing "Trading Spouses" a few times and
noticing how the extreme contrast between the two
families always made for a lot of drama. Usually they
would pair up a relatively gentle and laid-back family
with an extremely neurotic, fanatic, control-freakish
one. My DS Brian (11) and I were intending to watch
the episode with the crazy screaming woman, but when
the last trailerwas playing just before the actual
episode came on, Brian said "I don't think I want to
watch this after all, it looks like it's going to be
too violent." So we changed channels and didn't watch
it. It was a great illustration of the difference
between fantasy violence and reality violence.

Thanks! I think these will look different on re-reading as children
get older and moms and dads have more experience with unschooling.

For anyone who bought The Big Book of Unschooling, finished it, and
wished there had been some more, there's Moving a Puddle. The Big
Book was written all as a set of summaries, last spring and summer.
Moving a Puddle was written over the years from when Kirby was nine to
the time he was 18 or 19. It's a collection of almost all the
articles I had had published by newsletters and magazines up to that
point.

$15 and postage. Christmas is coming. <g>

Because of the realities of the post office, I can't combine the two
books by priority mail. It's less expensive to send them separately.

Because Moving a Puddle isn't as fat as the other one, though, you can
get two copies for the same postage cost, or add a copy of Thinking
Sticks with only one postage payment.
http://sandradodd.com/puddlebook

Sandra

I just had to deal with this at school. I was given by the school nurse
a bunch of forms to give to certain kids, who are missing some
vacination. The school nurse highlighted where the parent can sign so
the school can give the vaccination themselves. Amazing, I didn't
realize that this happened. THe parents don't even have to be present,
the school can just get the signature (and some parents truely don't
know what they are signing) and the kid is called into the nurses office
and the nurse gives the vaccine and the kid goes back to class. There
is a very tiny line at the bottom where the parents can sign if they
don't want the child to receive the vaccination but it is really hard to
understand what they are signing--if you don't speak English or don't
have a college education, you might not understand that all you are
signing is that you don't want your child to get the vaccine. I just
bit my tongue and gave out the sheet.

Joylyn

I still can't believe how this has changed my financial life! Several
weeks ago I read a message that provided a link to:
http://www.wrldofproducts.com I thought it was very interesting and
decided to check it out. I am very skeptical by nature so the first
thing I did was check out the company. I contacted the Better
Business Bureau and found that the company has had no complaints
lodged against them, I also contacted Paypal who guarantees the
purchases made by consumers with this company. They told me that they
verified the company's licenses, bank accounts and other paperwork
indicating to them that the company is legitimate and thus they
insure your purchases. Then I started asking co-workers and friends
about this program and to my surprise 6 out of the 10 people that I
spoke with were already using this program. They told me how pleased
they were with it and how much money they had saved and are currently
saving. So I decided to sign up for this program on 11/20/02. On
11/25/02 I received my complete membership package and the fun began.
Using their program and the free lifetime membership that it includes
I went to the supermarket ready to make a killing. The cashier rang
up my grocery bill and it came out to $ 239.87 then I presented her
with the coupons I received from this program and I saved $ 213.38 -
my final bill was only $ 26.49! The cashier wasn't the only one
shocked. I want to thank that person so much for this amazing tip!

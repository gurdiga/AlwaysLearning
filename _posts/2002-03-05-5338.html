In other events: We have found a property/community that we are
moving to later this spring for intentional community, for those of
you who were following our odyssey towards that goal. It is on the
Olympic Penninsula near Port Townsend, WA, and we hope to be there
before summer, or just as soon as we can get ourselves moved. Tom
will be commuting for a while, staying with family near his job
during the week and spending extended weekends (every Friday off)

employer is up at the end of this summer-beginning of fall, at which
time he will look for closer work to where we will be.

We are nervous, excited and anxious to finally be making the move.
We are following our dreams and taking risks in this, not doing the
expected thing and staying where we are financially safe in the life
that we know.

11. Don't pass on your own fears and hates about learning anything.
If you hate or fear math, keep it to yourself. Act like it is the
most fun thing in the world. Cuddle up and do math in the same way
you cuddle up and read together. Play games, make it fun. If you
can't keep your own negativity at bay, at least try to do no harm by
staying out of it.

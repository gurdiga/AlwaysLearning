I would say to put a timer on (for you, not for him) and either watch
him or get your son to watch him, and suggest a pausing place (a
saving place, or a still part of the game). Then say "Pause it."
And when he does, say "go to the bathroom, and then come back." If he
really doesn't need the bathroom, he could at least get a drink of
water and maybe a snack and then go back to it.

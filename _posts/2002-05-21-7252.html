Someone suggested Aveeno. You can also use just plain old oatmeal. When we
have a rash, we put oatmeal in a cloth and secure it with a rubber band.
Then hold the bundle under the water and get it good and soaked. Squeeze
the oatmeal water out over the itchy areas.

Afterward, the oatmeal is dumped in the compost pile and the cloth into the
laundry.

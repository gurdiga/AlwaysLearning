On March 18 from 1-2pm, the Mark Steiner Show (a public radio show based in
Baltimore) will do a program with Chris Bobel, the author of *The Paradox of
Natural Mothering* published by Temple University Press
(http://www.temple.edu/tempress/titles/1581_reg.html). The program will
discuss homeschooling as one of the aspects of "natural parenting" and will
also include homebirthing, extended nursing, and natural health care. Billy
Greer of the Family Unschoolers Network will be one of the guests for the
program.

If you are out of the broadcast area for the Mark Steiner show, you should
still be able to listen on-line by going to http://www.wypr.org/
Be sure to listen on Monday March 18 from 1 to 2 pm Eastern time and
participate by calling in or sending e-mail to the station (visit their web
site for contact information)

Billy Greer

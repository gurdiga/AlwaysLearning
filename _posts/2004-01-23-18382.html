And for those that don't mind a bit of blood and creepiness, the movie "From Hell" is another Depp film that is very well made. So if you don't mind a thriller that has some gross scenes, I highly recommend it. Depp plays a character so vastly different from Jack Sparrow (that's Captain Jack Sparrow actually:) it's a great way to see the range of his talent.

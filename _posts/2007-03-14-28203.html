Crazy Life Unschoolers: This is a support/idea group for those
striving to radically unschool and parent respectfully within a less
traditional family: singles, grandparents, foster families, adopted,
divorced, nannies, more than two parents, one or more kids in school,
etc., with a common theme being the need to rely on other, more
conventionally minded adults and the impact that has on our lives and
relationships.

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/crazylifeunschoolers/

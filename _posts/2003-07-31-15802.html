Kathleen,
I hope you didn't think I was complaining about newcomers, I certainly didn't want my post to sound that way! I enjoy answering questions about unschooling and discussing the finer details of gentle parenting.

As to my rant,
What gets me really annoyed is people defending practices that don't fit in with the goals of the list. "Spanking is fine in certain cases" was the most recent example. That's just fine if you want to feel that way and not change your mind, but don't spout off about it on a list whose goal is to help people get to gentle parenting, yk?
And this list is a place for people that want pure unschooling.
If you don't want it, FINE! But why do they have to come here and then feel the list "beat them up"? I wouldn't go to a vegan list spouting off about how SOME animal products were absolutely necessary and they were all bigots to say I don't need animal products, ever.

I guess some people have a hard time understanding the purposes of certain lists. This one is for radicals....easy enough right? :)

Anyway, seeing someone whine about this list was a bit more than I could bear. Forgive me for venting on you all.

Ren

cyrusnmayasmama at 2007-07-19 23:08:26 +0300 said:

Gene Simmons bought me my first legal drink at midnight on the eve of
my 21rst bday! I had gone to see a friend from high school (we were
both living in LA at the time) play with his band at the Troubador
(sp?). My friend was a drummer and Gene Simmons was his absolute idol.
Simmons happened to be there scouting talent and my friend couldn't
resist going up to him at the bar to tell him so. I was just standing
there and Mr. Simmons shook his hand while my friend gushed about how
he had influenced every part of his life etc etc. It was so loud that
his words were drowned out and Simmons leaned in and asked him who his
friend (me) was. So Jason introduced me and told him I was turning 21
at midnight. Simmons looked at his watch and it was midnight and he
leaned in and said "Happy Birthday! Please allow me the honor of
buying you your first legal beer" I said sure and we drank together
and pretended to have a conversation - very animated, just moving our
mouths and gesturing, because it was so incredibly loud! It was fun.
He was very nice and funny and very polite. Then someone came and
ushered him away. A Fun memory.

Alyse

NHEN's newest email newsletter just for NEW HOMESCHOOLERS:

N-H-E-N
New Homeschoolers' Encouragement Newsletter
http://www.nhen.org/newhser/default.asp?id=402

Each month, N-H-E-N will focus on particular questions that new
homeschoolers have been asking. If you're new to homeschooling, you won't
want to miss this monthly encouragement. If you're not new to homeschooling,
we'd love to have you offer your advice to the new families!

The theme for the September issue centers around a question that many new
homeschoolers ask themselves:

CAN I REALLY DO THIS???

Ann Lahrson-Fisher addresses the question with her article, "Your Trusty
Homeschool Emergency Toolkit." And homeschoolers from all over the country
want to reassure you that "Yes, you CAN homeschool your child!" Be sure to
read all their responses. As Denise Thompson from the NHEN Homeschool
Editors' list reminds me, "Choosing to homeschool may be a leap of faith,
but it isn't a leap into the unknown." So, let the the newest NHEN
newsletter show you how to get started!

See the newsletter in its entirety at
http://www.nhen.org/newhser/default.asp?id=402
Subscribe to N-H-E-N at www.NHEN.org (look in the left sidebar)

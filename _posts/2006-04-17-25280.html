> Two friends' children also have gotten clingy at eight
> ~Kelly

Interesting! We had a really cool discussion last night after reading
the stuff about rules versus principles. One thing led to another and
my dd8 volunteered that lately she's just felt the need to be with me
and do things with me as a way of recharging. This came at the time
when were discussing the differences between introvert and extrovert
because of some issues the kids have been having with fighting when
one wants to be alone but one wants to be with people. My dd8 said it
best when she described that she needed to plug into people like we
plug a lamp into the wall for energy, whereas my son needs to plug
into silence to recharge. :)

So, we laughed about how funny and interesting we all are and that as
a family we'll be here to support one another and meet each other's
needs. It's just so different from how I grew up and how I see other
families interact sometimes.

Beth M.

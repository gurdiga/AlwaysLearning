Today in the chat someone asked about games for a Game Cube because
they've bought one in secret for Christmas.

I couldn't remember the name of the game I was thinking of until the
chat was over and I stepped outside. I think it's a gamecube game,
and my kids had a lot of fun with it.

I asked Marty for advice and clarification:


For the 64 there was Smash Brothers.
For the Wii, there's Smash Brothers Brawl.

Marty says they've never made more than one for a system, so you could
just say Smash Brothers for Game Cube.


Mario Kart Double Dash (can be four players or any number less)

Lost Kingdoms is a one-player game (or two player).

There's a Harvest Moon game for Gamecube.

Zelda games are good, and you can get Ocarina of Time, or Windwaker,
or Majora's Mask if you like Zelda games.
Ocarina of Time is considered one of the best games ever made.


I recommend getting as many controllers as you have children, plus one
or two so parents or guests can play, or so there will be an extra if
one breaks. That's if you're going to get Smash Brothers or Mario Kart.

the Game Cube plays Donkey Konga, which is a music game with bongos as
controllers. That's pretty awesome.

Sandra

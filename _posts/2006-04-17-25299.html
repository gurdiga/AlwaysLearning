A couple of weeks ago I went to the electronic's store with my
husband. I choose to go but really had no interest in what was in the
store. It was BOREING! and it really made me think about how my kids
feel when they come say shoe shopping with me. If you have NOTHING to
do in the store and the person who is doing the shopping is jsut busy
shopping you are left. So if your are having a hard time remembering
what it's like to be in that situation (and that is pretty much the
situation most kids are in when you see them out shopping) Try it you
will be more interactive with your kids next time and when you see
other kids out "shopping" give them a smile and try with them too!

learning all the time!

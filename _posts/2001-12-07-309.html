Jennifer,

Thanks for the opportunity to plug it once again! I thought everyone knew
about it by now!

Columbus Day weekend (October11-13, 2002) in Columbia, South Carolina at the
Holiday Inn Coliseum. Sponsored by Palmetto State School's Out Support (SOS)
(an unschooling support group).

Our speakers include Lisa Bugg, Anne Ohman, Joyce Fetteroll, Ned & Luz Vare,
Fiona Hutchison, and ...Sandra Dodd (among others that I'm working
on---$$$!). It will be for the whole family with workshops and activities for
everyone. I'm at the hotel this afternoon to finalize some things. I'm
meeting with "Spiderwoman" next Wednesday, so we're hoping to have the
website up and running as soon as possible. I'll keep everyone posted!

If you're interested, please send me your snail mail address and I'll put you
on the mailing list. There are carpools/caravans being set up all over the
south and northeast! It should be a GREAT weekend! I hope you can join us!

Kelly

Live & Learn Unschooling Conference in Columbia, South Carolina. Columbus Day
weekend, October 11-13. The Holiday Inn, Coliseum---downtown.

Speakers include Lisa Bugg, Anne Ohman, Joyce Fetteroll, Fiona Hutchison, Ned
& Luz Vare, and Sandra Dodd. I spoke with Pat Farenga yesterday---and if
enough of y'all come, I can afford him as well!

There will be lots to do for the whole family with storytellers, magicians,
and MANY activities, so please bring the children! Weather is generally VERY
pleasant here in October---and the pool will be open for all you yankees!

I'm meeting with "Spiderwoman" tonight to set up the website, so it should be
up and running soon. If you send me your snailmail address, I'll put you on
the mailing list.
Thanks for your interest!

Kelly

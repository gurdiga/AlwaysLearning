I find a similar situation in my family and town.It is a tough
concept for people to grasp that learning happens out of school.
The thought that it happens naturally , unprovoked or scripted is a
jarring thought to most.I am faced with questions that are
Sophmoric.Such as what about the Prom? Don't you want them to
remember the PROM? I am very surprised when senior citizens or others
who are may have Not attended The Institution of SCHOOL, ask "But
how will they learn?" I respond,Humans have been on the earth for
millions of years,we managed to learn with out organized
schools,school politics and budgets for an awful long time; did we
not? Change scares many,many,people.

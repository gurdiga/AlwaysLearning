Eve is "passionate" about Peter Pan. The next morning there was a little pouch tied to the tree with mentos in it- (Daddy's favorite mints). She was tickled, and whispered to me that she thinks her daddy is Peter Pan. We have had a lot of fun with this-
I wish I could fly too!

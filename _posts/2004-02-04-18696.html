This list isn't for selling things. If someone has something to sell, MAYBE
if you can't resist, put a note saying "I have some things to sell, e-mail me
if you're interested," but three posts listing things with prices seems to be
something that will not make the over-400 list members more glad to be on this
list.

I don't want to make a rule against selling things.
I want list members to understand the principle that the list exists to
discuss unschooling.

I don't want to be unfriendly, but I'd like for others to consider the
friendly-factor when they decide how to use the list too.

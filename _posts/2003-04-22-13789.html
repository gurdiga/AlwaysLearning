Theatrix, Homeschool Community Theater, is delighted to present its

16th production, an original musical version of Alice In Wonderland on

Thursday, April 24, at 2:00 p.m.; Friday & Saturday, April 25-26, at

7:00 p.m.; and Thursday, Friday and Saturday, May 1-3 at 7:00 p.m. 


This colorful, entertaining adaptation of the enchanting children's

classic by Lewis Carroll will be presented at the Hobart Stage (46th

Street & Blaisdell Avenue South) in Minneapolis.  Tickets for reserved

seats ($4.00 for 16 and younger and $7.50 for adults) can be ordered

by calling 612-827-5995. For more information contact Theatrix at

www.theatrix.org.  Don't miss it! Also watch for Theatrix at the

Minnesota Fringe Festival in August.

An interesting note:

The song Kira wrote for the Mad Hatter's tea party scene, also the
show's theme song, has a rather unschooly chorus! It goes like this:


Pay no heed to your head.
Never mind your mind.
For if you're overly concerned
With what you have and have not learned,
You're going to waste some precious time

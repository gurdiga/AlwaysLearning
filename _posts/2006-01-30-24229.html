Tonight a friend of Kirby's was here. I don't know her very well;
I've just met her once before. She came down to watch Desperate
Housewives with me and Holly, until time for her to go out to eat
with some of the other older kids.

At the commercial, she said "I'm in love with your house!" I thanked
her, and asked if she had seen the whole thing, seen the back, and
she said yes, she had gotten the full tour. She was all big smiles
and said "It's the Weasley's House!"

I had a twinge of shame at the jumbliness of it all, but honestly I
love the Weasley house and wouldn't want to live in a Dursley house.
(I don't think I'll ever have to worry about that.)


Sandra

P.S. When they went out to eat, at Bennigan's, they took Holly with
them. That's pretty sweet. Marty was already in bed before 8:00.
He works early and hasn't been feeling well.

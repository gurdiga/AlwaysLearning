Vomit was originally Pizza - at least, they were trying to make a
pizza-flavored jelly belly and came up with one to try, but everyone who
tested it thought it was gross. So they shelved it for a while... then
when they started the Bertie Botts they remembered it, pulled it out, and
renamed it Vomit.

An interesting tidbit Rain learned on her last factory tour... she likes
to chat it up with the guides.

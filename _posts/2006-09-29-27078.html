Guy" then the 90% for the "Tie Dye Temple" table takes up 90% of the

the "Teeny Little Super Guy" table.

I used to be surprised by coincidences like that but now am more
amused and grateful.

Last weekend on Saturday, Marty and Holly and I were doing a five hour
+ drive, and we listened to an old cassette tape of an LP of Little
Shop of Horrors. The intro goes "On the 23rd day of September..."
and I'm like "HEY! What day is this!?" The kids weren't sure, but
I had the wedding invitation, and there it was... same day.

Somewhere in that happy sing-along hour, Marty asked who sings Audrey
II. I said Levi Stubbs, from the Four Tops, who wasn't usually the
lead singer, but sang "Bernadette."

Time passed, wedding, reception, touristing, swimming pool, and when
we started driving back late the next morning we were too far from
regular radio stations. Marty went "search, search," and found an El
Paso oldies station. The second song they played after we turned it
on was Bernadette, by The Four Tops.

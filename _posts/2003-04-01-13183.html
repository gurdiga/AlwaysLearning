Billy or Nancy

31 Mar 2003, at 7:46pm
Just a quick reminder that the MHEA homeschooling conference is this
Saturday at Anne Arundel Community College (about half-way between Baltimore
and Annapolis). Please come out to support MHEA. Since 1980, MHEA has been
the only statewide inclusive organization representing Maryland
homeschoolers of all religious views and philosophies of homeschooling. This
conference will have something for all homeschoolers, whether your children
are young or old, you just started homeschooling or have done it for
decades.

Go to www.MHEA.com for more information.

Also, FUN Books (www.FUN-Books.com) will be at the conference. If you have
any special items you would like to see us bring to the conference, please
let us know right away. You can place an order in advance and just let us
know you want to pick it up at the conference so you won't have to pay
shipping or have to worry about us running out of something you want. You
can also let us know about any items you would like to look at so we can be
sure to bring them with us.

Please come out to support MHEA and attend the many great workshops and
activities! Be sure to remind all your homeschooling friends about this
upcoming event.

Billy Greer
www.FUN-Books.com
(I apologize in advance if you get this message from more than one list)

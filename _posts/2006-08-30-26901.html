I had a wine prescription from my midwife also! Dh and I went through
a difficult separation while I was pregnant with Jalen (youngest
child, now 5.5) and towards the end of it, I was so stressed out that
it led to an environmental depression. Given my extreme emotional
state, she told me to drink a glass of wine "right now" and have a
glass every night if I needed to. It couldn't hurt the baby at that
point and my emotional state was more harmful anyway. I didn't drink
every night, but for a couple of days it felt like it saved my life.

His birth was really wonderful, and I have to think the wine did quite
a bit to help.:)

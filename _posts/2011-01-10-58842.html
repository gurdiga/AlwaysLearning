I thought of the ones on here who have asked about things for children that have a passion for all things war / battle and wanted to share something our son Zachariah found.

Zac discovered a new Manga series called "Hetalia Axis Powers" We found it at Barnes and Nobel but I just looked and Amazon has it too. In fact volume 2 just came out on Dec 28 so we are ordering it. It is rated older teen age 16+

I'm guessing from reading it that it is rated older due to the ability to "get" some of what they are bantering back and forth about. Not sure otherwise as I didn't see any "adult" stuff!

They use anthropomorphism - giving human characteristics to the countries. I really enjoyed reading the short personality synopsis they had for each country.


He said it is also in Anime form but that he had trouble viewing it on-line due to the web site constantly asking him if he was 18.

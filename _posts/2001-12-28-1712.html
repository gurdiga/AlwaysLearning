Yes, but it would be all scratchy (and expensive, unless you had friends with
home equipment, but those old enough to own turntables often don't have CD
burners and vice versa). I'd check Amazon or other such places and see if
it's been released on CD, OR if it's available from one of the historical
outfits or custom-CD places. I just got Holly a CD of selections from the
1920's and 30's, from a place online. www.americanmusicclassics.com
and they might have some Shirley Temple; I didn't look.

Holly got interested in the music in Paper Moon, and so I ordered her sixteen
things from the 20's and 30's:

They charge something, then $2 per song, and shipping, so it's not cheap, but
it's cheaper than buying a whole CD for one or two songs (and several others
for the rest).

239FP Let's Have Another Cup of Coffee

2494E IT'S ONLY A PAPER MOON

2492CE MA, HE'S MAKING EYES AT ME

202SE Shine On Harvest Moon

233DP We're In The Money

15A I Wanna Be Loved By You(#43)

240KB Cheek To Cheek

2972F JOURNEY TO A STAR

248WF Got A Date With An Angel (radio, 30's)

4878FL I'm In The Mood For Love (TD Spotlight Bands)

240FP Love Makes The World Go 'Round

4879FL Stormy Weather (classic 50)

5822M TAKE ME OUT TO THE BALLGAME (SPOTLIGHT ON)

236DN Deep Purple

8307MS Loch Lomond (Classic 50)

241AT It's A Sin To Tell A Lie

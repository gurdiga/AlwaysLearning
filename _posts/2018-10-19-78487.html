I love Game of Thrones. The show is very explicit, both sexually (nudity, allusions to rape) as well as war, fighting, some torture, and outright butchery. Though I have seen them all, when I re-watch, I will skip certain and scenes. I don’t need to see them again. 

It depends on the individual. My husband read the books and loved them, but after episode 1, he won’t watch the others. Too graphic and it stays with him. 

My kids have not shown an interest, but I think 11 is pretty young for this. Again- depends on the individual and their sensitivity- but I would prob put my 12 year old off for a couple
 Years, or watch after having prescreened so I can warn of the especially rough parts. I did this with sister in law who is very sensitive to abuse and violence toward animals. 

If you are concerned about the sex, then simply watching the first episode will give you a clue. It gets more explicit from there-

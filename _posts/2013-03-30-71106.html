An email address is like a phone number. It doesn't matter who you get the address (or number) from. They work the same. A Yahoo address will work on Google groups and a Google address will work on a Yahoo group.

The big differences are in how good the spam filters are and how easy it is to read. (Though there are programs like Microsoft Outlook and Apple's Mail that download mail onto your computer.)

You're using AT&T which should allow you to add more email accounts. The downside of using an email from a provider is if you change providers, you need new email addresses.

I don't think there's anything special out there for kids but I let it through just in case someone knows.

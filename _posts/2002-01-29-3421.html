To me an enriched environment means full of resources to satisfy passion.
My passions are history, science and education so I make sure I have
what I need to satisfy them. I have a television, cable channels,
internet access and I subscribe to email lists which discuss the topics I
find interesting.

My children are passionate about many things; art, music, reading,
pretend, video games, etc. I, out of a passion for them, try to provide
those things which satisfy them; art supplies, musical instruments, books
and frequent library visits, dress up costumes and props, video games and
consoles.

It's so natural really, I love to see them not just happy but zealous;
excited about whatever they're doing. For me, it's a no brainer, I
acquire those things that I can please them with and thus please myself;
a win-win situation.

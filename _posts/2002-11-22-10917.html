I would have said the same thing back when all my kids were anglo, but now

Some prejudices are so deeply ingrained, particularly in language, that I
catch myself. "Nigger-rigged" is a common term here for something that is
put together is "bailing wire and spit". I was grown and married before it
dawned on me what I was saying about Black people when I used that term and
trust me, nobody that knows me would think of me as prejudiced.

I don't have a clue if Tuck is prejudiced, I don't think we can ever know a
person's heart. What I do know is that when I read her statement about
cultural differences, I thought "I wonder why she thinks its a cultural
thing rather than a personal thing."

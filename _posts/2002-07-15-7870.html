Thank you, Sandra, for the link to your article about your sister's family.
It's Mary here who has had a child now ask for school. She has been home,
unschooled her almost 8 years. She's going in for an insiders veiw she says.
And added, I'm going to school for fun, everyone else has to go.

This fall will be unique. Ashley will start 3rd grade at the end of August. My
2 other little ones will be here home. I suspect our days will be spent riding
Ashley to school, playing at home, the park, and visiting local places like
the firestation, then picking Ashley up. It's like stepping back 2 years when
my oldest was 5. Time will move slower for us at home and I look forward to
that. Ashley keeps teasing me that I do not want her to go to school, which I
answer back,"I want you to go to satisfy your need to go, I just don't want
you to stay there very long". She has said now she thinks she will go to
school for at least 2-3 years. I'm hoping for a few months, yet again, this is
her choice, she's making it and I'm, gulp, supporting it.

Mary

We all got fishing poles and are practicing our casts. Next year maybe will
put hooks on them and try fishing for real.

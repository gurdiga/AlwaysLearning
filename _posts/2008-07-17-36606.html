I started a list for unschoolers who like manga and anime. All ages
welcome. :-)

I know there are unschooling conferences coming up and I thought it
would be a great way for kids to meet before the conference and maybe
have some already in place friends before they get there :-)

Otaku Unschoolers
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/otaku_unschoolers/

Either go to the website and sign up there or send a blank email to:

otaku_unschoolers-subscribe@yahoogroups.com

Feel free to pass the link along to other unschooling groups (or
groups sympathetic to unschoolers) :-)

Joyce

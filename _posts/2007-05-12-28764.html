Almost all of the unschooling boards *I* know of are secular. The
bigger boards all are. Although religion does come up occasionally the
moderators consistently work to keep those discussions directly
related to unschooling (as Sandra did, here, just recently).

As far as finding a "good fit" - some of that is just personalities,
but there are boards with specific purposes - like U. Basics for newer
unschoolers, or Always U. for folks with younger children.

> Sandra's blog is neat because her blog roll lists recently updated blogs. (wish I knew how to do that!)

Hey, Jenny, you can do that through your blogger dashboard. Go to "Layout" and then choose "Add a Gadget". Choose "Blog List" and then add your favourite blogs. You can choose how much info shows up on your sidebar. On my blog, I choose just the blog's title and the title of the latest post. And I choose to list them according to recently updated instead of alphabetical order. Have fun. :) :)

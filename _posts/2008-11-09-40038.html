Robyn L. Coburn at 2008-11-11 11:57:50 +0200 said:

Yes, to my pleasant surprise I was invited to be in the book. I always
imagined my short essay as part of series on Rules vs Principles, because
there have been several very strong pieces written by others including
Sandra and Danielle Conger (previously published in LLM) and Ben Lovejoy's
writing on the topic (that was in Connections amongst other places). I think
the book looks quite nice, I'm proud to have been included - and yet I would
also encourage anyone reading my bit, to think of it as a jumping off point
to the even better writing of others still floating around the net.

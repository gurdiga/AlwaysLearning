>>I would love some links for radical/alternative/liberal bumperstickers
>>Does anyone know of any?>>

We made the "Free the Children" one ourselves at makestickers.com. It's got
a red circle/slash with school in it (as in "no school"), and
unschooling.com under that. There's lots of places online. Head shops, if
you know of any, are also a good place to look. I got a cool one at a little
head shop in the middle of nowhere in Wyoming. It says "Learn from others,
but go to your own school". Those two are mixed in with my namaste, Darwin
fish, yin yangs and sun,moons and rainbows. Like I said, I decided to pretty
well mark myself when I moved to Utah. Bumper stickers don't hurt like
piercings and tatoos. ;-)

I have a 13 yr old stepson who loves Runescape. We don't limit him -
but we currently have four people sharing a computer *and* it's dial-
up. We're looking to expand those options, but in the moment we do a
lot of communicating and looking for solutions that work for
everyone. One of those solutions has been dss shifting to a more
nocturnal cycle - its his choice, and one that appeals to him in
other ways, too (creature of the night and all that).

That involves a bit more planning for some things - today he's going
to the skatepark with some friends, so he's asked me to wake him up
in time to go do that. I'm also making sure he has "breakfast" that
he can eat in the car. I'm also working on budgetting my energy
differently, since he's waking up around the time I usually start
lagging :(

What aspects of Runescape appeal to Jonah? Ray loves the social
aspects. He seems to spend a great deal of time sending and
receiving messages!

Just as a side note - I know there's a unschoolers guild for World
of Warcraft, is there something similar for Runescape? The dial-up
is keeping us off WoW for now, but I think Ray would enjoy touching
base with other unschoolers.

---Meredith (Mo 5, Ray 13)

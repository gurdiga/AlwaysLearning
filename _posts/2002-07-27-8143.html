There are more sordid things you might want to avoid. The first page of
choose your own sex act is this, and having an iMac, I like the second pickup
line :

Your saga begins in a commercial establishment licensed for beverages,
alcholic, the sale of; and intended for socialization, interpersonal, leading
to sexual encounters, classification desperate, sub-classification shameful.
In other words, a pick-up bar. And lo! There's a member of the appropriate
sex who fits your minimal standards of attractiveness and personal hygiene
now! What well-practiced opening line do you use on him or her?

* "If I made an obnoxious, sophomoric pun about your body, would you hold
it against me?"
* "Much like the iMac, I come in five colors."
* "You look like you need a friend"


Come to think of it, that's not as bad as the first page of the Pokemon
adventure:

   
You are Big Bertha, novice Pokémon trainer. Yous is your life's ambition. You
don't have many friends. You have arrived with two Pokémon of your own,
Legless (a squirrel-like thing with no legs) and Yuckipoo (a rabbit-shaped
creature who likes to fling poo). You are standing on the beach, what would
you like to do first?

* Dig randomly in the sand
* Look under that out-of-place rock
* Go back to the ship and see if anything's hiding on board

And some here might appreciate "Operating Instructions for your THX Mark II
Lightsaber" http://www.brunching.com/lightsaber.html

Sarah at 2011-05-17 14:33:07 +0300 said:

I have a 3 and a 4 year old (boys). Popular in our house:

Peppa Pig (really very good app)
Dora the Explorer (rainbow ride- good for colour I think)
Monkey Lunch Box
When I Grow Up (dressing up game)
Fairy Trails- very clever- uses the camera so you can hunt fairies and butterflies in your own living room
Angry Birds, Cut the Rope, World of Goo etc are popular and good for playing together.

He may like the Big Green Rabbit songs on youTube: search for 'Green Anaconda Song', 'Spider Monkey Song' and you'll be led to others. Mine also love the dinosaur song, one thousand steps, umbrella bird and the iguana song.

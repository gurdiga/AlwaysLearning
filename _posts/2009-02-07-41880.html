A big annual event we attend has always had a raffle that was a lot of
trouble to administer and took way too long to call out prizes etc.
This year they introduced a new kind of raffle. Everyone who bought a
$10 raffle ticket got a red envelope which contained a prize number,
which they took to claim their prize. Everyone was guaranteed to win
something. Our kids loved it.

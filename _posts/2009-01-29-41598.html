have had some responses on the other one; that's fine).

The notes from today's chat on young children will be here:
http://sandradodd.com/youngchildren

With a 20 month old, I think carry her as much as she wants to be
carried, let her walk as much as she wants to and not a step further.
I hate to see people holding a kid who wants down, or telling a baby
"you can walk."

Find lots of things she can touch and pick up and hold. There will be
lots of suggestions at the link above pretty soon, too.

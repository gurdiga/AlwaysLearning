In the words of E Ray Moore, good friend and
president of Exodus Mandate:

"Now Stossel and 20/20 are set to puncture the last myth of
complacent suburban
parents who delude themselves by thinking that, whatever problems
there may be
elsewhere, their schools are "different." As Stossel and 20/20 point
out, not
only do our 12th graders do worse in math and science, for example,
than students
in almost all other industrialized countries, the longer our
students are in
public schools, the worse they do in relation to their international
peers."

is certainly adept at making up new myths when the
public sniffs the rat in the current myth du jour.

>I'm Jamie and I'm a homeschooling mom in Michigan. I homeschool my
>oldest of 2 girls, and will one day school the little one as

>
>I am currently the moderator of a homeschool shoebox swap group,
>where each month members that would like to particpate are paired up
>with another family somewhere around the world. We then gather
>information from our home state/country, things like postcards,
>newspapers, rocks, maps, sand from a beach, etc... and then we
>exchange them with our swap family. It's a fun group, and neat to
>learn about a new place, while at the same time, learning quite a
>bit about our own state and/or country. We are a list of 81
>homeschooling families, and at this time are putting the word out to
>other homeschooling families around the world in hopes that they
will join us.
>
>I was wondering, if it's okay with you, if you could please pass on
>information about this group to your list? We would love to see
>our group grow. It's a fun way to learn a little about the world
>around us, and to meet new homeschoolers around the world.

Well, when we moved last fall, we finally decided
(after one finger painting + rug incident during
bedroom painting, and several other mishaps) to move
the kids and I first. DH finished up the painting,
repairs, cleaning, and packing, and then came along a
few weeks later. We canped out in the apartment at
our new town with some air mattresses and folding
chairs, kids table to eat on, folding bookcase and a
few essentials until he arrived.

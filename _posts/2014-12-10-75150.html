I'd like to add (without telling a long back-story) that providing a generous expression of love along with each serving of food may help a great deal too. Insecurities about love and belonging are often expressed through our relationships with food; as he heals from a real lack of food it will be valuable for him to begin to recognize that your love and desire to care for him is there in abundance as well.

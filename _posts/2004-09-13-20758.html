This weekend Shahram and I spent a lot of time going over our financial situation and setting up a budget. Margaux heard most of our discussion.Late Saturday night, she came to me asking how much her gameboy recharger costs per month when it's not being used. I know that there is some flow of energy happening in everything even when it's not turned on. I didn't find the information on that yet, but we found this. There's a great list of home appliances and how much they cost to operate. You just fill in the amount of days they are in use, or the hours, and it shows you how much you're paying. This is very cool.

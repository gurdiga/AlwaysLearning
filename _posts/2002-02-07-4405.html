Hubby and I are working on doing the same thing as Heather and her husband.
I started my business so that I didn't have to dump my kids in daycare...
Hubby quit a full-time salaried with benefits job to work on a contract
basis to get used to the idea of being self-employed. My business is
getting to the point where I can nearly sustain the entire family. The next
step is moving to a bigger house (we won't qualify for the mortgage once he
quits his contract) and then he's going to quit and work with me in my
business. I'm working on a contract for a syndicated radio show so I'll
need him to help with tons of things. That way we'll both be home with the
kids and I'll work from the studio in my home. It's just taken
perserverance (sp) and as desire to have the life the way we want it.

himself how to trade futures & options. He turned his hobby into his own

move to self-employment part of his de-schooling process.

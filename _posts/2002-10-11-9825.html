When I attending music school -- one of the biggest thrills was to go the the listening library and check out/watch the Bugs Bunny Barber of Seville video tape. The opera majors especially!

There is a childrens orchestra production of "Bugs Bunny on Broadway" -- the Philadelphia Orchestra did it this past summer. From what I understand, it travels to different cities with the same conductor. They show Bugs Bunny cartoons on 2 or more screens while the orchestra plays the music live! Fantastic Music Education!!

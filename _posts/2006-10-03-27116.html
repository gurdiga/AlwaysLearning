In my daughter Vivi's search for all things barbie, she came across these zombie dolls. She's also a horror movie fan, so they're just perfect. Each one comes in a coffin with a death certificate. We've ordered one and she's hugely excited! She was keen on having a go at repainting a barbie's face, and now she's probably going to create a living dead barbie!

If you click on "Morticians" in the left side menu, you can read the story of the creators. It's a cool story about following a passion and how that was supported by parents.

Does anyone else have these dolls?

I answered you privately.

I'm tired and have other interests I want to pursue.

It's important to understand that *many* things---not just unschooling
conferences---should be taken advantage of while you have the
opportunity. There is never a guarantee that ANYTHING---or
anyONE---will be around another year. Take the time---MAKE the
time---to experience things when you *can*. You may never get another
chance.

There are PLENTY of other unschooling gatherings now. Lots. LOTS! Just
not Live and Learn. But almost every area of the country (as well as
Costa Rica and Canada!) has something to help you enjoy other
unschoolers. If not, start one!

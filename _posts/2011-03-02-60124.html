Although it wasn't let through to the list, some of you might have received an e-mail about someone going to London and being mugged and needing money. It's a scam (and maybe an automatically generated one), and it's popped up on and off for years.

Please don't respond to it.

Sorry to put this on Always Learning, but I would hate for anyone to lose any money (or computer security) over it.

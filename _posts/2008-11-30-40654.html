Definitely check out boardgamegeek.com for detailed information on
this
game. You can find user comments and ratings, tons of pictures of
the
game setup & being played, forums about the game that include
reviews,
rules questions and strategies. Here's a link to the specific game:
http://www.boardgamegeek.com/game/103

This game is great for those who enjoy adventuring and monster-slug-a-
thons. Also remember that it can take 2-4 hours to play, depending
on
the number of players, with a minimum age of 12.

Other games he might be interested in:

Warcraft: The Boardgame - not as highly rated as Titan, but takes
less
time to play, costs less - http://www.boardgamegeek.com/game/7479

not to be confused with

World of Warcraft: The Boardgame - also very good
http://www.boardgamegeek.com/game/17223

Age of Mythology: The Boadgame -
http://www.boardgamegeek.com/game/6707

Risk: Godstorm - http://www.boardgamegeek.com/game/10383

Talisman (4th edition) - http://www.boardgamegeek.com/game/27627


So that should get you started. I encourage anyone who is interested
in boardgames to use the massive information that boardgamegeek.com
has
available, it can really help in making the right decision when
considering a game to purchase. I've been a user on BGG for 6 years,
as well as an avid boardgame player and collector.

I see that you were shopping at Cardhaus - they are wonderful people
there in Lynnwood and truly have the best prices in this area.

Other online sources:
boardsandbits.com - good prices, cheap shipping, but sales tax
thoughthammer.com - good prices, no sales tax, free shipping over $125

Feel free to contact me directly if you have questions :)
GOOD LUCK AND HAVE FUN!

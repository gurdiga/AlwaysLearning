5. It doesn't matter when something is learned. It is perfectly all
right for a person to learn all about dinosaurs when they are 40
years old, they don't have to learn it when they are nine. It is
perfectly all right to learn to do long division at 16 years old,
they do not have to learn that at nine, either. It does not get more
difficult to learn most things later; it gets easier.

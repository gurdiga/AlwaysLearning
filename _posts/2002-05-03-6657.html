We watched a cool show the other night, on Discovery or World Link or
something or other like that. It was called "Kantum Diary". It was about a
bloody battle in Viet Nam during which an American soldier found a diary of
one of the enemy soldiers. The diary haunted him for decades. He finally
goes back to Viet Nam and meets the man who wrote the diary. They become
friends and it is a very healing process for both men. Good show.

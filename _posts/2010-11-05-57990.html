Shawn C at 2010-11-08 07:56:15 +0200 said:

Hi Jennie. My two oldest sons went to iD Tech Camp for about 4 years and loved it! They had a blast and learned a lot. It is mostly run by college kids with some older adults in supervisory positions. They went near us in Ann Arbor to University of Michigan.

Another great camp is at Michigan Tech. My middle son has been going to that camp for three years. It's quite a drive up to Houghton from Ann Arbor, but well worth it. My youngest son and I go, too, and have fun in the area for the week.

Have fun finding just what Pearl will love!!

Shawn

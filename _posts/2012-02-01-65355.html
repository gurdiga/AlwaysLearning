Holly said this morning "I'm really looking forward to this test today."

After her driver's ed test and the placement test at the college, this is the third test she's taken.

She's in a math review class. There are many others. As far as she knows, she's the only one who hasn't been to school before. One person is a youngish grandmother (18 year old daughter had a baby since class started).

I asked how important the test was, and she said there are four or five tests (I'm guessing the last one is the kind you can skip if the first four are good scores, but she doesn't know that system, and I didn't ask), and it's a pass/fail class. THEN she said what surprised me. You can retake each test two or three times. "They really do want to get people through that class," she said. :-)

Easiest test-taking situation ever in the world. And she's looking forward to it.

That's a nice experience for her. :-)

For a long time if some of you remember my son was so very scared of
other kids at the park. He wouldn't want to stay at all if there was
any kid he didn't know there. The same happened when we went to a
store,he would freak out if someone looked at him. My husband was very
concerned,he said it really bothered him,Lukas might need preschool or
something...blah blah. I reassured him that it will change. We didn't
do anything except honor Lukas' feelings. We did not force or talk him
into any social situations he didn't want to be in. He did change. He
is so social now that my husband said one day that he is starved for
social scenes. Speaking of trick or treating last night....it was
Lukas' first time(he wasn't into costumes before) He rang the
bells,talked to people and had a great time. My sciatica didn't bother
me. It was nice. So for all you parents worrying...don't :)

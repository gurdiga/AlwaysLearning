I'm formerly from N. Idaho, and my husband was born
and raised there...and although it is beautiful, can
you say LEAD POISONING? ;) Not a great place to
raise kids--at least along the I-90 corridor. Further
north, like Sandpoint, is very nice, and further
south, like Moscow, is also nice, although that's not
called "north" idaho, at least unless you're from
extreme southern idaho! ;)

We lived in Challis for a few years, and it's nice
there, if you don't mind -40 winters. Salt Lake was
out nearest "big city", although it was 6 hours or so
away, and we would go holiday shopping there every
year. If you are looking to get "away from it all",
Challis is perfect (200 miles from a mall!)
Dana

My kids, ages 4.5 and 6, like to play around here: http://www.shredderchess.com/play-chess-online.html. When the player clicks on a chess piece, the game highlights the places on the board where that piece can move. They're starting to really get it, just playing around.

They've also played some of the games here: http://www.learn4good.com/games/board.htm. I don't have flash at the moment, but if I remember correctly, the "crazy chess" game was lots and lots of fun.

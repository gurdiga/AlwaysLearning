In my Holt readings I found he recommended a wonderful book, "And the Children Played" by Patricia Joudry. I got it as an inter-library loan as ours didn't own it and I read it aloud on our camping trip this weekend. It was funny and warm. We all enjoyed it, my 10yo especially, and it isn't a "children's" book at all. Very well written.

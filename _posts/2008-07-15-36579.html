Hey everybody,

I've always thought that sites like Myspace are neat but too gargantuan,
and I went ahead and built a site, an online community for unschoolers:
www.unschoold.com

It's a place to blog, engage in unschooling discussions and connect with
others. Currently, members can create groups and organize meetups.
I've been kicking around the idea of chatrooms, classified ads (how cool
would it be to support other unschoolers!) and more. My dreams are only
limited by my time :-)

I'd like to invite everyone to join community and shape its future.


peace,

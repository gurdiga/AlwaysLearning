This is the text from the list welcome/guidelines (posted on
Yahoo).

Since we have so many new members who may have joined
through other avenues than the front entrance, I thought I'd better
post this so everyone's clear on what the purpose and
guidelines for the list are:

Discussion for homeschooling fans of John Holt, whose books
Learning All the Time, Never Too Late, and Teach your Own have
made unschooling a sweet and viable option for thousands of
families.  

This is a moderated group, with trapdoors for the uncooperative.
(Not moderated in the advance-approval way, but in the be-nice-
to-play way.)   It's an idea group and is intended to lean more
toward pure unschooling than neutral, general homeschooling
discussion--there are hundreds of general homeschooling
discussions for newcomers.   It's to focus more toward how
people learn no matter where in the world they are, rather than
on what's legal in any particular country or jurisdiction.  


If you have questions or complaints, write to

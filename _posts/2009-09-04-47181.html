One thing it says there is: "This is a moderated group, with
trapdoors for the uncooperative. (Not moderated in the advance-
approval way, but in the be-nice-to-play way. New members' posts are
moderated, and it's good to read several dozen posts before jumping
in.) "


DO ask questions about unschooling.

We don't need introductory posts. Information in the course of other
discussion is fine, but an introduction for the sake of an
introduction isn't as helpful for a discussion list as for a social or
support list.

"Thank you" and "me too" and "LOL" are assumed; don't post those
unless it's in the course of useful comments that will help others
understand unschooling.

Don't write rude letters to the moderators nor to me. Be with your
kids. Gather what you need to know. Don't be abusive of me nor of
any moderators.

Getting angry doesn't mean you need to leave the list. It means you
shouldn't have gotten angry. Figuring out how to be calm is part of
learning to unschool.
http://sandradodd.com/parentingpeacefully

Probably the questions you have have been asked before.
A great deal of information for new unschoolers is here:
http://sandradodd.com/help
http://sandradodd.com/beginning

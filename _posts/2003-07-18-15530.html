Hi, been reading for awhile, haven't posted yet. I read this and had a
memory flash. Have to share.
I was on the toilet and... the phone rang. My son who was then 3 decided he
would answer it. I called out not to bother but I heard him dragging a chair
across the room towards the phone. I sat there helplessly and listened to
him answer the phone and tell the person that "Mummy can't come to the phone
coz she's doing a poo!" Aagghh!!!!! He came in a few minutes later to tell
me it was Noni (my mum). I called her when I could and she answered the
phone crying from laughter. Phew!

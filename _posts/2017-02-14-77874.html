Though it wasn’t intended, I’ve created a sort of “Best of” Just Add Light, which you can see by going to this blog and scrolling back a couple of years. :-) (that’s not very much, for this blog—I only post maybe once or twice a month in general)

http://aboutunschooling.blogspot.com

A few years back, I started adding a link to a Just Add Light post and its photo. So each time I have three pages ready to announce, I go through recent posts and pick one I think is one of the best of the past week or two.

The blog itself announces pages new to my site, or pages with additions people might want to catch if they’ve read the page before.

None of the newer pages (“newer” meaning seven or eight years) is in the randomizer at my site, but this blog has a randomizer. I have a batch I’m still (slowly) working up to add altogether, but randomness is fairly easy to come by, either at the blog above or at
http://sandradodd.com/random

If you get bored, explore and discover new ideas to fortify your mind and soul for unschooling joy. If you see a great quote somewhere, and you know it’s not in Just Add Light (which you could discover by searching for a distinctive phrase), maybe send it to me to include. Positive quotes are better than negative, naturally, for a cheery daily blog post. :-) But I know there are hundreds of good bits that haven’t made it to Just Add Light and Stir yet.

http://justaddlightandstir.blogspot.com
(If you’ve subscribed, clicking the title of any post in the e-mail will get you to that blog, and there’s a randomizer there, too)

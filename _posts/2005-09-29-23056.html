Anybody know about the PEAH card? I was looking at the Joann's
discount card and they are now requiring you to be a PEAH (Parents
Education at Home) member. You can get a Joann's access code for free,
but to really be a member it costs $19/year (on special). Meanwhile,
it looks like their online directory of other businesses is not yet up
and running, although they claim there are 150+ of them. Are there any
other businesses requiring this membership? Has anyone had any
experience with it? Check it out: www.PEAH.com
Gretchen

Yes. Our local group complained loudly about this. In Michigan you do not
have to go through PEAH to get a JoAnn's discount card. You can get one
right at your local store. Not sure if that is just Michigan or if it
applies everywhere.

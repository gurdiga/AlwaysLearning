Hi Sara,
I hope someone will actually post with some accounts of unschoolers getting into uni in the UK, but I wanted to share my own story of how I got into Uni in the UK after failing all my GCSE's and leaving school at 16, as I imagine similar paths are available to unschoolers....

I decided I wanted to go to Uni in my mid-20's, and the local college offered what was then called an 'Access Course' for mature age students (which I believe was anyone over 21). It was the equivalent of doing your A Levels in one year, and allowed you to then apply to Uni through the UCAS system. I was accepted to Aberdeen University, my first choice, after finishing my Access Course :)

That is just one potential route into Uni, i'm sure there are many others.
Cheers
Jo

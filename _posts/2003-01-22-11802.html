More dads help with homeschooling
Wednesday, January 22, 2003 Posted: 9:45 AM EST
(1445 GMT)


CHICAGO, Illinois (AP) -- For Tim and Nicholas
Peebles, school is always in session -- even at the
grocery store.

As the father and son stroll the aisles, Tim finds
plenty of visual aids to teach 8-year-old Nicholas
about subjects like health and nutrition,
agriculture and economics.

No longer content to sit on the sidelines, fathers
like Tim Peebles are getting more involved in what
was once firmly a woman's domain: homeschooling.

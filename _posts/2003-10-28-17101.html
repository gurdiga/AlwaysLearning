I'm on a generic state homeschooling list and every once in a while I throw
in my unschooling 2 cents. :) Or should I say Unschooling Sense.

Last week we had someone asking about kids going back and forth between
school and home and wondered if anyone had gone through that. Their
previous attempt at homeeducation hadn't flown. I shared my story, our
kids went back and forth a bit, and then suggested she check out different
methods of homeeducation. Gave her some links--to unschooling.com and
others.

That same day I got a couple off list e-mails asking me about unschooling
and also had 3 people join my unschooling list!


There are days though that I do feel like its an uphill battle, like when I

list, and they are doing their 'schooling'. Ugh! That's why noone is

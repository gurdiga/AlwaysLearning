Have you tried hotwire.com and priceline.com? If he's flexible about the
times he leaves and arrives, they often save a lot... hotwire will just
show you the prices, and priceline has you put in your "bid" and then you
wait a half hour and see if you got it. Priceline also adds fees now...
and you can also book hotel rooms through both, and specify the area and
how many "stars", minimum. We got a really nice room throught priceline
when we went up to see Karin in Phoenix, I think it was a Howard
Johnson's, walking distance from the play we were seeing, and only $30.

1 Jun 2007, at 11:46am
I wanted to thank everyone who helped me work through my little spell.
(I had been having thoughts of enrolling my kids in school for a few
weeks to give myself a break and satisfy their curiosity.)

Many of your comments really set me back on the right path. Especially
what Pam said about how it's not at all helpful to have thoughts of
school in the picture at all, even as a "last resort." So true! I
realized it's like getting married and on your way up the aisle
thinking "If this doesn't work, I can always get a divorce." It may
be true, but it's not going to help you commit to having good marriage
no matter how difficult things get.

I had never had any doubts about unschooling before, so it was kind of
a scary thing to go through. Your responses really helped re-energize
me. I knew I could count on you!

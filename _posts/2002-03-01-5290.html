Actually, the party is in northern California, but non-northern
Californians are welcome to attend ;-)

You are hereby cordially invited to the Spring on the Lane- Brownold Farm
/ Welcome Home Dar and Cacie party on the fram near Dunnigan, California.
We'll start around noon on Saturday, April 6 and end sometimes Sunday
afternoonish, April 7. A group of NBTSC kids will be spending the week
visiting, so in-house sleeping space is pretty much taken, but the
weather will be gorgeous and there's plenty of room for pitching a tent,
or you can stay the the Best Western in Dunnigan, or make it a day trip,
or if we unpack enough boxes by then we *may* have some room in our
house.

We're about 2 hours from San Francsco or an hour from Sacramento, near
the intersection of Highways 5 and 505. Generally everyone brings a dish
to share, many games are played far into the night, and everyone is free
to roam around and play with the dogs, chickens, barn owls (who don't
really play back), tire swing, sandbox, etc., etc., and to talk and talk,
and often knit although it's not required. We guarnatee many interesting
people...

I swear we're not spamming every list with invites, just two, so you're
guaranteed not to see this more than twice. I'd love to meet anyone who
can make it , and if you're interested but can't make it this time, we do
pretty much same thing every Septmeber.

Email me for more info,

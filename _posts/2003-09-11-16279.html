I like http://www.pcpitstop.com for troubleshooting my computer. It has a test that is free that will test your computer and tell you what you need to tweak to fine tune your hard drive. The entire site is free. There is a pop-up ad for a free tune-up that is not free. Don't get confused by it. It also has a forum where you can ask questions or just read all the advice given to others. It doesn't matter what software you have, the geeks there know them all.

I'll have to check out Geek.com. I have a favorites folder for sites regarding my computer, but I've never been to this one.

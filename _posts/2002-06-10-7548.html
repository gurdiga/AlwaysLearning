does seem to be one important difference. People with CFS are likely to
exercise-intolerant, whereas people with fibro tend to be helped by careful
exercise. Possibly it's a matter of which symptom tends to be the most
bothersome. For me, even though I'm diagnosed with fibro, the biggest
problem is the fatigue.

I couldn't resist sharing this one. I was commenting to my son the other
night that even though he was happy when we took him out of school he just
seems to get happier and happier with unschooling. And this was his
reply...



"Mom, I'm like a fish that is drawn to an angler fish whose bait is
homeschooling." Ds 7 years old



For those of you who don't know, an angler fish is one of those weird freaks
of nature that live in the deep ocean and have these strange limbs that
dangle in front of their mouths and light up to lure other fish.



Yes, the analogy does break down a bit if you think on it too much, but I
thought it was so cute.

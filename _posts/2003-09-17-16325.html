I've created a new Yahoo! group for people unschooling with serious
mental illness, called Unschooling and Serious Mental Illness. Here's
the description:

This group is for those who are unschooling and dealing with serious
mental illness in the family.

Often we find that as unschoolers we have a different mindset from our
more mainstream family and friends. This is a forum for getting together
with those who share a philosophy as well as a challenge.

We'll be available for support and advice, as well as just being able to
hang out with someone who really does understand what we're going through.

The link is: http://groups.yahoo.com/group/UnschoolingMI/

I'll be posting this to all the new HEM lists and to Sandra's list, but
if you're on another Unschooling list, would you help spread the word,
please. I think this list will fill a real need that many families don't
know how to address.

Thanks!

Diane

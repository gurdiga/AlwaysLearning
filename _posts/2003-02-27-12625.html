Sounds like so much fun, Pam. We've been having great spring-like weather
here lately. Really, truly, amazingly nice. Last year at this time, it was
raining every day and we were inches deep in mud. So I'm doubly grateful
every time the sun comes out in the morning.

Unfortunately, my son has been fighting a stomache bug, so going outside to
play was out. But we had a great day, too. Tim still had some fever, but
was feeling so much better today.

We sorted all of Tim's coins into the boxes that you sent (thanks!),
labelled the sections, and made a list of all the countries he has
represented (88, in the final count!!!). And we spent hours sitting in the
sunny living room paging through coin books trying to identify three elusive
coins without obvious country names on them (Morocco, Tunisia, and Croatia).

We took breaks to watch all the videos he's almost outgrown and hasn't seen
in ages. I haven't had this much fun with a sick child, ever!

brenda

First you need an emulator for the game system. There's some great Mac ones
at:

http://emulation.net/index.html

(If anyone needs emulators for PC's the best ROM sites should have them or
have links to sites that have them.)

Then you need to download the ROMs (games). I think there's some links at
Emulation.net that I started with. And then used the links on the best of
those to find other sites. The emulators are machine specific. The ROMs are
universal so you don't need to look for ROMs for the Mac or ROMs for the PC.

(From what I've casually read the disk based game machines (Playstation,
GameCube, X-Box) and games don't emulate very well.)

The sites tend to come and go because the best become too popular with too
many people downloading so the domain owners shut them down. And some sites
exist solely for the purpose of generating ad revenue and are entirely popup
ads with a list of other sites (with popup ads.)

But here's some links I've used that had huge selections of ROMS:

http://www.emureactor.com/
http://www.emulatorworld.com/index.php
http://www.neogeoplanet.com/
http://www.sn1ck3rs.tv/
http://emulation.darktech.org/
http://www.dgemu.f2s.com/Home.shtml
http://www.britroms.com/
http://www.emubox.net/

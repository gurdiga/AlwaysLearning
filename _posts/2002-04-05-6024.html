So... this weekend is the official Spring on the Farm Party - Wecome Home
Daron and Cacie Party - Meet a Bunch of NTBSCers Party - and Just Hang
Out with Other Homeschoolers of the Unschooling Persuation Party. There
will be food a-plenty, bring a dish to share and just hang out. Possible
attractions include searching the sandbox for small animals, finding the
barn owls, watching the chickens, petting the rabbits or dogs or cats,
singing Monty Python songs, playing The Great Dalmuti and other games,
swinging on the rope swing, building a lego universe, and many more.

We're located outside the town of Dunnigan, about 60 miles north of
Sacramento (or 160 miles south of Redding), right off Highway 5. It's
about an hour and 40 minutes from Oakland or just under an hour from
Sacramento (or 22 minutes from Woodland and 32 from Davis, if you drive
like I do). People tend to begin arriving around noon on Saturday and
leave easrly Sunday afternoon, but some people are coming Friday, we're
flex. Indoor sleeping space is taken, but there's plenty of room to pitch
a tent, and we'll even lend you a tent!

Email for direction!

Pam,

There is an option to moderate new members only. Anyone can still
subscribe, but their initial messages will not go to the list until
approved by a moderator. If their first message is spam, you can then
ban them from the group without having the message go through.

I am "no mail" status for this list meaning I only read the posts on-
line which also means I cannot see the e-mail address of the spammer.
The moderator or any one else actually receiving the message by email
should forward the message to abuse@yahoogroups.com and abuse@
whatever the domain was for the jayma818@m... (all that shows when
you read on-line) address.

Those steps should get their yahoo account closed so they can't use
it to spam again and will get the response address closed so they
can't benefit by receiving replies to their spam.

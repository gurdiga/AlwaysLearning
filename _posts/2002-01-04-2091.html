I like the way you think Kelly. We are wonderfully
immersed in the holidays in that sense as well. I
LOVE to decorate with Santas. Santa is real in the
same sense that dreams and visions are real and
important to us. When our kids are older we will be
discussing with them the importance of embodying for
each other (and others) the virtues of Santa. Santa
is very real in a spiritual sense. We ALL do
stockings here, mommy and daddy too, not just the
kids.

I also love to point out the pagan beginnings of so
many various traditions. It's so much fun to watch
the faces of people that don't understand what we mean
by "pagan" when they come over and see our tree, and
santa's and candles and decked halls and hear carols.
But, it's all one, one web, one energy, one Higher
Power, so it doesn't matter whether the chicken or the
egg comes first.

Nnaci K.

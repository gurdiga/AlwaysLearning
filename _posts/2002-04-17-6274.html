Hello,

My hubby and I have gone down the "they need to be doing school stuff" road
before. What worked for me was to put it on him. He could do school with
them if he wanted too. You can cram a whole week into Saturday and Sunday
mornings. We do beleive we learn anywhere we find an interest. Maybe your
hubby needs to set a learning goal for himself and babystep it until he
reachs his goal. I also agree being around other confident unschoolers helps
sell the idea that yes our kids are always learning. I'll forward my hubby
good posts to help him be connected to the unschoolers I gain so much from
also.

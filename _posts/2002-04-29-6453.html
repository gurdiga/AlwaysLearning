Dear Fellow Homeschoolers,

My name is Mimi and I have been homeschooling my 7 kids for the past
14 yearsway back when people did not even know what it was.

In 1997 every parents nightmare came true for my husband (of 23
years) and I lost our beloved Andrew to an unnecessary and
preventable death. Rather than become debilitated by our devastating
loss, the Lord gave me the hope that He would use this tragedy for
His glory. Immediately following Andrew's death, another son, Riley
Josiah, became critically ill and required life support, 24 hour
nursing, and 26 operations in 2.5 years. During this time of mourning
our loss, caring for our sick child I was caring for a newborn and
homeschooling 4 other kids. The Lord taught me two invaluable
lessons - not all loving parents are able to homeschool by themselves
and that there was an enormous need for a homeschooling help to come
to parents when they cannot do it all.

To fulfill this need and God's promise that our loss would be used
for good, my husband and I founded The Grace Academy, an online
school (pending licensing) that delivers world class curriculum to
children throughout the world.

Many have called The Grace Academy the "best of both world-
homeschooling and Christian private school." Grace provides a
comprehensive, world class Christian K-12 curriculum, daily Bible
studies, devotions, and dedicated Christian teachers - all while your
child(ren) learn in your safe and nurturing home.. Grace's curriculum
uses many of the leading Christian publishers like Bob Jones,Alpha
Omega, and Saxon Math. Choose from over 150 courses that all have a
balanced Christian world view.

The Grace Academy provides everything you need to provide your child
(ren) with a thorough and standards based education. Our interactive
technology allows student and teacher to communicate directly in real
time. Our faculty will mentor each student individually through out
Live Tutor technology.

Information about Grace can be found just a click away at
www.thegraceacademy.org.
Demo our courses, browse through our course catalogue, read our
mission statement and sign up for a free library of over 1300 etexts
on CD that we will send to your home as a blessing to you for
visiting our website.

The Grace Academy provides outstanding curriculum where students can
learn at their own pace, anytime, anywhere, any path.

Leave your children in the hands of Grace.

Abundant Blessings in Him,

Mimi and Howard Mandel

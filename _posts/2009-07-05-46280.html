I'm listing them here because there are a couple of formatting
glitches on the site itself, but the collection is cool.

These blog carnivals are a good way to get a cross-section of some
different blogs and writings that you might have missed. I enjoy them
because they're a mix of people I know and people I don't, but writing
about similar topics.

The real site for it is here, and it doesn't require special writing
but can be a post you've already written.
http://familyrun.ning.com/forum/topics/here-we-go-julys-blog-carnival

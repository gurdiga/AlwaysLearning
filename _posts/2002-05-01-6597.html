In Florida, if we are registered with the county we live in, we are
required to have either an approved test, such as SAT, or we can have
our children evaluated by a certified teacher.

Since this year was my first year off the curriculum I was planning
to go with the teacher evaluation. My husband is currently working in
a ps and has met up with a friend he went to high school with. We
were thinking of asking her.

My oldest daughter "had" a big desire to go to school. So, right
before Christmas break my husband had a party for his classes. We let
our daughter go with him for the day. Well.... to say the least...
she has made it perfectly clear that if we send her to "that place"
ever she will leave home!

I was thinking of joining one of the umbrella schools with only the
requirement of attendance. Do any of you have any experience with
them? What have you found that works well when you are unschooling?

Rebecca

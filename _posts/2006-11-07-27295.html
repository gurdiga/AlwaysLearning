The best way to see how other unschoolers are doing it is probably to
read the archives of the list (go to yahoogroups.com and you can get
there, and to follow some of the links from here:
http://sandradodd.com/help

Those go to lots of articles for beginners (your mom might enjoy
reading some of that) and to other people's unschooling sites and
blogs and photos.

There are some other discussion lists there too that might be more
appropriate.

Sandra

It's a good idea *not* to open messages with attachments from someone
you don't know very well, unless you requested it. The other day I got
a message from an old acquaintance with a subject line that said "we got
a new rotatiller", and it turned out a virus had sent out that message
to everyone in my friend's address book, and my antivirus caught it
before it infected my 'puter. =8-O My antivirus did not key up on the
message to this list, so it could indeed be safe, but I don't take any
chances.

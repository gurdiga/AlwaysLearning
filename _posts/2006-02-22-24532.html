Hi All,

I am Laura. I am a christian unschooler. I have been married 11
years as of this last saturday! We live in Oklahoma City. I am
originally from Oklahoma, but just got home in December from a two
year move in the Houston, TX area. I have been unschooling since the
beginning, although I didn't realize it until about 4 years ago. I
have 3 kids. I have a boy 8, girl 5, and a baby girl 1. I am so glad
to be a part of this awesome group! I have learned a lot already
from lurking.

A friend of mine and I just started a new yahoo group tonight for
christian unschoolers in the OKC area. If any of you are here in
Oklahoma, please come and join us too on a local level! We are
wanting to get together with people that are in our area as well as
online! I look forward to being a part of this group!

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/unschoolingchristiansinokcmetro

I finally was prodded properly (by Cindy Fox -- Thank you!) to look
into domain names. And now people can type in:

joyfullyrejoycing

into their browsers and it will go to my website. (As will the fuller
versions:

http://www.joyfullyrejoycing.com
http://joyfullyrejoycing.com

of course.)

Let me know if anyone finds any problems with it.

The only thing you need to remember is to misspell rejoicing as
rejoycing but I've typed rejoycing into Google in the past to see if
that would be a quick way to get to my site and found that lots of
people don't know how to spell rejoicing ;-) I can't spell it right
anymore either!)

The old address that no one could remember will redirect to the new
host but I may drop Earthlink at some point so you may want to make
the change to any links you have now. Just replace:

"http://home.earthlink.net/~fetteroll/rejoycing/"

with

"htttp://joyfullyrejoycing/"

I've been in Australia for a month. I wasn't able to get to all the chats, because noon at my house (when the Wednesday chats start) is 4:00 or 5:00 in Australia (I've been in three time zones). 4:00 in the morning, Thursday, for a Wednesday chat, I mean.

So because the chats have regular attendees and various drop-ins, three nice people agreed to be "guest stars."

Marta Pires has (quickly and generously) edited all the transcripts.

This Wednesday/tomorrow I will be in the chat (from a motel room at 4:00 a.m. Queensland time), and next Wednesday I will be in my own front room or kitchen, in Albuquerque. I have retreated from human companionship to hide by myself and sleep more and not need to talk to anyone or to think clearly, until I can recover from a month of more humidity, insect bites and socializing than I could handle. I told Keith that I know he needs time alone every day, but apparently it takes me 25 days to need time alone. :-)

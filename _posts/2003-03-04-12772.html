My son was talking this morning about Bush and the potential war with Iraq
and other issues it was just so fascinating to me to hear him think. He is
someone who thinks everything out loud. He would be talking about an issue.
Doesn't really matter, here, what his point of view is or what the subject
is. I would present the counter argument. Or just something to add to the
mix for him to think about. He would pause for a minute to think about that
and then start thinking out loud again. It is just so wonderful to see him
processing all the information and formulating his own ideas and opinions no
matter what my opinion on the subject might be. Very political and
fascinating.

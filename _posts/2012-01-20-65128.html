This video is a talk given by Jane McGonigal, a game designer, and her
ideas on how games give us the experience of problem solving and help us
gain optimism about success. She believes that gaming is an evolutionary
skill that can be used to solve real life problems. I thought her ideas
could be use to some folks here, especially in the gaining new perspective
on the value of gaming but also provide information on some new gaming
resources.

http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dE1DuBesGYM

From the library we checked out "The Secret of Roan Inish." (I saw it several years ago when it came out, but my 10yo was a baby.) What a wonderful film - the grandparents care for a motherless girl. They're very loving to her and she's on a mission that unfolds for her as the film progesses. School is never mentioned and the girl and her older male cousin accomplish something amazing through hard work, persistence and their own calling.

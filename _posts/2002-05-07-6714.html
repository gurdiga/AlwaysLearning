We used to have a BIG problem with ticks when we first moved here to Clovis,
NM. They were all over the dog and the kids every time they came in the
house. We finally had to treat the whole yard with diazenon.(sp?) We did the
back yard first, and wouldn't let the kids or the dog back there for a
couple of days, then did the front yard. No more ticks since, and it has
been 4 years since we treated the yard.

I made a sling (not a snuggli) It's easy. Just hem the outsides of a
rectangle *aprox*. the same length as your daughter's arms, fingertip to
fingertip.
Fold one end through two craft rings (can be plastic, wood or metal) and sew
closed. Done.

To wear, tuck the free end through the rings and pull to desired size.

Hi,

I recently ordered and read "Homeschooling Reflections" by Connie Colten. I
believe Sandra Dodd wrote a comment for the back cover of the book,
recommending it. At any rate, I truly enjoyed the book and wanted to let others know
more about it.

As I said to a good friend last week, there are so many families I admire
when I see how their families appear to function and how the children behave --
eager to learn, interesting and interested in life, and, insecure person that I
am (that likely many of us are), I wish I could be a little mouse in the
corner and observe one of these families on a daily basis for a while to really
see how they function, day to day, hour to hour.

And that is what I really enjoy about "Homeschooling Reflections" -- the way
Connie Colten allows us into her family in such an intimate and honest way.
And what I learned was not the secret to perfect homeschooling or unschooling,
but rather, just that it works, not perfectly, but best, when we trust our
children.

If you are interested in learning more about this book, you can visit
Connie's website:
www.homeschoolingreflections.com


Diane Shelton

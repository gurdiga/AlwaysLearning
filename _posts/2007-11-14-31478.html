I think you're talking about the free version of wordpress,
www.wordpress.com .. you can't alter the themes without paying
something. You can choose from a lot of themes though and not all of
them will have the big type. You can make pages and title them as you
want. Wishlist, Books, Recipes etc ..

There's also www.tumblr.com. You are given the option of posting text
(which could be your lists), video, chats, pictures, links (or this
could your lists), audio and quotes. I am not sure if you can have
individual pages but it's more scaled down version of a regular blog.

They are both free.

Yes this is "Time Team" that plays on History International (part of the
Discovery stable of cable channels) but not as that title, so sometimes it
is hard to find. I have seen it under the banner of "History Traveler" I
*think*. (It's been a while.)

I love this show. It's like a game show for Archeologists, with a time
limit. What I love about it most I think is how delightedly excited they all
get when they realize that their theory is WRONG and they have to revise and
rethink. They employ a mixture of high tech searching methods and ancient
artisan techniques for reproductions.

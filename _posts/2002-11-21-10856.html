There are so many forms of hatred in the world it's sad. Any of the "isms"

India the other day because they fell in love and got married. Their crime?
They were of different castes. The people who killed them were their own
family members. Hatred and lack of tolerance comes in many forms.

Sometimes living in a multicultural community such as we do here in Abu
Dhabi is a strain and yes it's easy to get mad or frustrated when "those"
people behave a certain way (you'd have to see this place to understand how
many cultures are packed in together). BUT overall it's fascinating and
enriching and I know my family and I are so lucky to be experiencing this.

Stereotypes often do come from very real things. However understanding and
then accepting the reasoning behind those behaviors is what moves us all
closer to tolerance and peace.

We did a Buggy tour when we were in Lancaster county a few years ago, and the
Mennonite driver told us a lot about Amish and Mennonite beliefs. Quite a few
Amish people have phones in little booths OUTSIDE their homes. In addition,
they can ride in cars, just not drive them. The kids are (generally) schooled
in private one room schools through the eighth grade, then they are home
working and learning more important stuff. Their homes are often pretty
luxurious, complete with convenient appliances using power that is not
attached to electrical power lines.

Amish don't use computers generally, though.

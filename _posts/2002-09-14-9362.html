This is the counterpoint to my other post.
The coolest thing is that Julian and I went to his Movie Premier!
Last October I had read about a film maker, Andrew Mudge, who was looking for
kids to be in his movie, The Perfect Gooseys, mostly as extras. Julian got to
do it. Mudge's previous film had been accepted to the Sundance Film Festival.
This one was just screened at the Telluride Film Festival (and will be in a
bunch of other ones too. He missed the deadline for this year's Sundance, and
is hoping for next year.)

This week they had a premiere of the movie in the Boston area! You can see
Julian three different times (once from behind, running down a hill),
including singing, twice! They also had a DVD for sale, so we got that.

So...my son is a movie star!
Kathryn

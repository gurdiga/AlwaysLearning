I guess I'm a diehard unschooler because I intend to hold firm to the opinion

---------------------------------------

Maybe someone here posted this, I don't know.

But I do hope that some of the great writers here who haven't been on
www.unschooling.com for whatever reason will consider going there from time
to time to help new people understand it.

It's fine for us to have this list, or other list, where we can word-wrestle
and tell stories with people who are already convinced and comfortable
unschoolers. I like it. But for any of you who remember how it felt to be a
new unschooler or who realize that something you say could make the
difference between a family putting kids back in school or keeping on making
them "do their work" or changing their relationship entirely, please check in
once in a while to help us help newcomers.

Thanks.

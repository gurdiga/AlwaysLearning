Tawni Sherman at 2003-12-19 23:10:04 +0200 said:

When we needed to move our daughter out of her car set and into a booster
seat, we did a lot of research. I felt best about the Britax line--was able
to find them locally (SLC), but I know they also have a web site. The model
we bought works for children up to 100 pounds. It's basically a
belt-positioning booster, and it's really easy to use (moving in and out of
the car, etc.) Good luck, and I hope everyone's fine after the accident.

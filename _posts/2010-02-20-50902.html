We are brand new to unschooling and definitely still in the de-schooling phase (well, at least I am anyway). The other day I over heard my son talking with one of his schooled friends. The boy was trying to wrap his head around unschooling and asked "Well, if you don't do math or writing or science [altogether now] what DO you do?!" Without missing a beat my son said "Three dimensional art." The freind looked at him wide eyed and then said "Cool!", paused and then said "What's that?" My son smiled widely and answered "LEGO."

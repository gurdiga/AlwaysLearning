Cacie and my dad and I saw a play about mathematicians last night (it's
called Proof, and it's really more about the interrelationships between a

the songs they did.

lower-case i. We all stand there and do nothing for 3 minutes - it's an
imaginary number!"

I saw it coming, but I had to explain it to Cacie during intermission...
then she liked it.

Loved the link to Donny Osmond doing his white guy dance during the
entire White and Nerdy song.
http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GWc6QQ9JlMc&feature=related
for those of you who missed it.

Donny really brought it! Did anyone else notice him signing ROTFLOL??
(At one minute 55 seconds.)As any true Osmond Brothers fan knows, he
has older brothers who are hearing impaired. I was SO in love with
Donny when I was a youngster. (I was in love with Jay too.) Tiger
Beat posters were all over my bedroom walls.

Loved the Bob Dylan palindrome video too!! For palindrome fans, Jon
Agee writes great palindrome books, one of which is called "Go hang a
salami! I'm a lasagna hog! and other palindromes." His books are kind
of like comics, they tell the story of the palindrome. Lots of fun to
look at with the kids. One of my faves: Eva, can I stab bats in a cave?

Jen

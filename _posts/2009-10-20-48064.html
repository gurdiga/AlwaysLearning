Special one-hour Yi-Tan Call 248 – Unschooling –
Our primary and secondary educational systems are controversial, to
put it mildly. John Holt, Grace Llewelyn, Paulo Freire, John Taylor
Gatto and others have done a fabulous job pointing out how deficient
the existing systems are, as well as lighting a path forward.

One growing alternative is unschooling. Unlike the popular home-
schooling stereotype of parents sharing “classroom” duties at home,
unschooling is mostly student-led (and I wince using the word
“student”). Explaining it in any depth is beyond the scope of these
lightweight invites, so please join this call and explore with us.

We’ve invited a wonderful group of deeply experienced unschoolers,
adult and adult-to-be, including Tammy Takahashi, Sandra Dodd, PS
Pirro, some of our Yi-Tan regulars and their kids.

With this awesome posse, let’s discuss:

• What is the range of unschooling practices? How quickly is it
growing?
• How does the rest of the world respond to unschooling? Is that
changing?
• What are the main benefits the Net brings unschoolers?

I love Kenneth Brannagh. Mostly because he loves Shakespeare. <g>
It's not physical attractiveness, although he's not repulsive.

But I really loved the moment in the new movie when he's signing autographs
at the bookstore and pulls Harry up for a photo op. I was thinking YES!
Younger Harry Potter fans will see him, LIKE him, and want to see what else
he's been in, and they will watch Henry V! They'll watch Much Ado About
Nothing! (Maybe they'll watch Othello too, especially if they're Matrix
fans, and that might be too much for Harry Potterites; and they might watch
the really long Hamlet, and that's too much... )

But the thought of kids watching Henry V because they liked Harry Potter
struck me as a worth side effect of that photo opportunity which works in
both worlds!!

I clicked through to Sandra's cool art links page where I was
reminded I told Sandra I'd try to find the Ambigram Generator I'd
stumbled across once. I figured I might as well share it here :-)

http://ambigram.matic.com/ambigram.htm

Ambigrams are words written in such a way that they read the same
right side up and upside down. NOON is a natural ambigram.

It's *very* clever that they could design a program to do that.
Though they aren't nearly as elegant as the human generated ones! :-)

Scott Kim is a master at them. His book Inversions is *very* cool and
includes words that turn into other words when turned upside down.
His website is:

http://www.scottkim.com/inversions/

If you type ambigrams into Google Image search you'll see over 2000
of them:

http://www.google.com/imghp

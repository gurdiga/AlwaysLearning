I have been on this list for quite a while and have only been reading
messages to gain knowledge about unschoolers. We have been unschooling in
Michigan for 4 years. We are the only unschoolers in our area and I have
been wondering what other unschoolers are like. I have the feeling that we
do not exactly fit in with the homeschoolers in our area.

I have hesitated to send messages to any groups, having the feeling that I
am not as intelligent as many of the great moms out there who do an
excellent job letting their kids learn freely. This low self image was of
course caused mainly by being forced to attend public schools for my entire
childhood. It is amazing how well into our adulthood we still struggle
with deprogramming ourselves.

When I read this message, my initial thought was, "What an unusual
thread. Unschoolers really are nuts, and I'm one of them!"

Then I started thinking about it and realized, this finally IS a message I
could reply to, and I DO fit in.

Anyway, tomorrow my husband and I are celebrating our 20th anniversary and
was wondering if anyone had any good suggestions for fine dining

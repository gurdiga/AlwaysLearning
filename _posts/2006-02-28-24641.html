Hi everyone! I'm Noelle, unschooling mama to three young children (ds5,
dd3, dd2). I actually have never read any of Holt's books, or any other
books on unschooling for that matter. When I was pregnant with my first
child, I knew I would homeschool. About a year later, I heard about the
unschooling philosophy and immediately knew it was for us. I was so glad to
have heard about a concept that seems like it was made just for me and my
family! I often reflect on how much I wish I had been unschooled as a
child. :) I've never been one to conform, so for me, there was never any
pressure to please anyone else. (We're an AP vegan family, so we're used to
not being mainstream!) I work from home so I can be there for my kids, and
I wouldn't have it any other way! I run a reading club and literary
activity magazine for children. (I'm always excited to receive suggestions,
so if anyone wants to recommend their favorite children's novel as a reading
selection, please share it with me...email me offlist with your
suggestions!) I'm very glad to have found this list of like-minded
families!

Noelle
http://literarychild.com

On Mar 1, 2006, at 11:48 AM, Literary Child wrote:

> On an unrelated note...any idea why some of the groups messages I'm
> receiving are coming to me in ultra-tiny almost-impossible-to-read
> font??

It might be because you're set to receive emails as HTML.

Try going here:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/AlwaysLearning/

Click on Edit Membership. (Below Yahoo Groups banner and above
"Always Learning" banner.)

Click on the radio button that says Do not convert to HTML.

Click Save Changes.

If that doesn't work, then it's either a temporary Yahoo glitch or
your email program. You should be able to set the default size in
your email program's preferences. There should also be a keyboard
combination that will increase the size. Check under Edit or View for
something that says Font, Increase Size.

Joyce

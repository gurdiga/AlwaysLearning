Yesterday a friend of mine called and told me about an accident involving
her van. She came home from the store with all her kids and groceries
and parked in her driveway. Her kids piled out and she took her toddler
out of the car seat and carried him in the house. While she was inside
her van rolled down the driveway, across the road and into the ditch. It
was quite a steep slope so there was some damage.
She said she ALWAYS leaves the toddler in the car seat until she unloads
the groceries but this time, God must have told her to take the baby in
first. Because of some genetic trait I speak before I think and I said
" Why didn't he just tell you to put the emergency brake on?".

A few day's ago I called my sister to ask her to look for fabric for me
in her garage sale-ing and thrift store shopping. (I make braided and
hooked rugs) She said, coincidentally, a friend of hers who's moving out
of the country had been there that morning and given her fabric. (she
makes dolls) How convenient!

Yesterday my mom was here and said she'd been out shopping at the second
hand store for a long wool coat. My sister's package arrived while she
was here and there was a note from my sis stating her friend had also
give her this long wool coat but it was too small for her and maybe mom
would like it.

So I said to Dylan, ~ our little coincidences are just like on "Signs"
with all their weird coincidences...How Graham was a religious man to
give his family faith and how Colleen died so Merrill the slugger would
move in with them and how Morgan had asthma to protect him from the
aliens and Bo had the weird water thing because the aliens hated water,
yada, yada and was it really a miracle and God did all that? And because
of some genetic trait Dylan said "Why didn't he just send them all on a
cruise?"

Deb L, atheist, coincidence believer and crop circle lover

We were taking the kids to the movies last night and dh chose the new Looney
Tunes movie over Cat in the Hat based on reviews. We all give it a thumbs
up! It reminded me of Who Framed Roger Rabbit; that kind of in-humor, so
many visual jokes spinning by that I'll have to get the DVD to find them
all. It was funny that just before the movie I was telling ds about this
thread and all the product placement in Cat in the Hat. At one point in the
Looney Tunes movie (which was otherwise fairly free of product placement if
you don't count Acme) they find a WalMart in the desert. As they leave, they
say "It's sure nice of WALMART to give us these WALMART beverages in
exchange for saying WALMART so many times!"

Anyway, skip the Cat and go see Bugs. It's mindless fun, although
school-at-homers might want to review pointillism and Salvador Dali. :) I
think toddlers through adults would enjoy this one!

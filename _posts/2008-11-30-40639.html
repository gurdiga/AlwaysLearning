It's called the "World Magic Awards". It looks like it was broadcast
on UHF stations so you should be able to catch it next year.

Google video shows the awards show going back to 2006:

http://tinyurl.com/5h6v75

The quality on some might be a bit better than YouTube (though some
just link to YouTube).

(Though I see magicians claiming various awards going back further,
but maybe it wasn't broadcast in the US.)

If you go to "My Network TV" (which is what the group of stations is
called) you can sign up for their newsletter and they'll let you know
when it's coming around again. Apparently they played the 2007 awards
again last April.

http://www.mynetworktv.com/shows.php?show=79

It might be worth checking over the next couple of weeks:

http://www.hulu.com
http://www.joost.com
http://www.sling.com

They're all legitimate portals for TV shows now that networks put up.
The quality there should be very good.

Dear Sandra,

I want to say thank you.  I made a donation and here’s why:

Your site, and carefully curated community of contributors, has given me untold support and food for thought over the past few years.  I know no other families ‘thoughtfully' unschooling in our area, and genuinely wonder where my thinking would be without this group. I thank the universe for the internet often!  

My friend went to see the Dalai Llama at Glastonbury festival this year.  He talked about parents, and peaceful parenting, being the only practical, real, route to world peace.  The future of humanity lay in our laps, he said.  This line of thinking, to me, seems to be rooted in common sense not dogma.

Your site has helped me and so many others to parent more peacefully and more joyfully.  I also feel able to pass on peaceful and joyful ideas to other friends who do not unschool or read your site. Therefore your site directly enables us to contribute to world peace (and to have so much fun in doing so!).

Could there be anything more important?  Hmmmm, nuthin coming to mind right now!

Thanks again, 

Belinda (UK).

PS, I love the uncluttered nature of the Yahoo Group, I find Facebook a bit overwhelming and distracting and tend to instinctively avoid it in general, so i do really value this space.

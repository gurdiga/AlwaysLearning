Not to try to pick out "non-writers" from writers, but I know there
are people here who read and read for years and don't write. There
are people who don't come to the chats because they don't type fast
enough to keep up. I understand that. If there was a weekly
unschooling water polo get-together I would not be there at all,
because I don't know which is worse--water? or balls coming toward my
head.

For a project LOTS of people will see for years, though, photos of
unschooled kids are needed:

http://unschooling.info/photosneeded

There's a link and some details. News this morning is this:

"Any pictures are okay - doing stuff or close-ups - all fine..." and
it's okay for there to be more than one of each child. Send as many
as you want.

It doesn't matter if they're current. I sent some from when the kids
were little. All that matters is that they're not stock photos, but
are real images of actual unschoolers. It's for Unschooling: the
Movie, Lee Stranahan's project.

And for those who want to come to the chat, there's one later today.
http://sandradodd.com/chats/regular
and another one on Friday, for which I'll be in Fort Collins,
Colorado, at Jill Parmer's house. I'll look just the same in the
chat, though.

Thanks!

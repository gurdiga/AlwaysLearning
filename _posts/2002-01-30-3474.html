On the SCA Homeschoolers list, someone had asked about dyslexia and
unschooling, and another mom wrote this:

<< We have learned, that it is possible for a parent to learn pretty much
everything they need to know to accommodate a child's disabilities, but it is
never possible for an expert to learn everything they need to know about a
child. >>

Now THAT I like. It's a way to cover the 'you're the expert' in a meaningful
way.
You have the opportunity to be the expert on your child.

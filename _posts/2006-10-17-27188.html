Fright Night: Music that goes Bump in the Night (allegro con fuoco)

THIS IS COOL. I found it because I put in a CD I own and told iTunes
not to import it, but when iTunes came up it showed classical albums,
down along the bottom. I clicked on this one to see what it was.

It's 34 songs, with a booklet, for $9.99 and I'm getting it. It's
not only Halloween soundtrack, it has recognizably familiar things
that will bring back all kinds of memories.

I wouldn't recommend using it to go to sleep at night, or for calming
peaceful music when someone's depressed. But for occasional special
use or a Halloween party, it seems perfect.

Most songs can be bought individually, too.

What's most cool is that its release date is October 17, 2006. It's
just new today! Had I not woken up at 3:30 I couldn't have told you
about it. <g>

There's one listed on Amazon by the same name, but it only has twelve
pieces, the cover is different and the reviews aren't great. This
has almost three times as much music for the same price, but I can't
send a link because iTunes isn't like that. Sorry.

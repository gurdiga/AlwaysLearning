I was wondering, if you had to use 5 sentences to describe what unschooling
is to you, what would they be?

WIldflower



1. The freedom to be true to who they are, all the time, at peace with themselves, happy, loved and nurtured.

2. Free to discover everything and anything that interests them - everyday.

3. Limitless and independent in their thinking. Making their own decisions in exploration of the world.

4. Family time is limitless, not constricted to 4 hours in the evening competing with unnecessary homework, reading and bathtime. The kids play with one another, feel closer to one another. This week alone my kids ( dd 7, ds 10) have laughed so hard they peed in their pants at least 4 times!!!!

5. They are each so happy to be home with mom. I have heard this many times from each of them and have been the recipient of hugs and even an unrequested kiss on the cheek from ds!! WOW

Since reading some of John Taylor Gatto, they are NEVER going back!!!!!!


Carole in CT - Mom to Lauren (13) Jonah (10) and Julia (7)

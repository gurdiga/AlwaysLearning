From the list: Central Texas Intentional Community:


This group is for the discussion and development of an Intentional
Community in Central Texas. The focus will be on raising our
children with the concepts of Taking Children Seriously (TCS) and
Unschooling in a communal living situation. Interested parties will
have at least one adult member of the familiy be earning an income.
Single parents are welcome. Members will be interested in
intentional community living and raising children without coercion.
Members will either already have children or will be planning to
have children at some point AND be pro-homeschooling/unschooling,
thus familiar with the concepts of child-led learning, and TCS. Join
us in the discussion.

from: http://www.ic.org : Intentional Community is an inclusive term
for ecovillages, cohousing, residential land trusts, communes,
student co-ops, urban housing cooperatives and other related
projects...

Go here to join the discussion: http://groups.yahoo.com/group/CTCom/

Tomorrow/Monday afternoon March 30

Wednesday afternoon, April 1

Friday morning, April 3


Both days, chats on unschooling. For the exact time in your time
zone, go to the link for the chat below.

I don't always announce them here. If you want regular reminders,
join the chat list that's linked from those pages (click the chat icon
at the bottom of any of the pages).

http://sandradodd.com/chats/regular

http://sandradodd.com/chats/being

That second link, the Wednesday chat, is on the topic of *how* to be
with a child, spend more time with a child, have a good relationship
with a child.

"For parents who feel most comfortable alone or who haven't had much
to do with other than their own children thus far, unschooling
requires a different kind of relationship and more involvement. Here's
a chat on connecting more with our children, and creating a strong
bond after infancy when the relationship changes and children's needs
expand more and more into a wider world.
"Having more ways to tap into unschooling success can mean avoidance
of school, and most important, avoidance of developing a distance
between parents and children. So if you can share ways of relating
better with your children or if you're a person who wants to know
more, come help in this chat if you have time."

Sandra

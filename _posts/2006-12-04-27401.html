I would like to introduce myself to this group.
I am a 34 yr. old unschooling mom of 4-- dd-11, dd-8, dd-6, & ds-4.
I love being with them (most days!! LOL) and helping them as their guide and example (working on that!)
I believe in my heart unschooling works, but do occasional battle with the old "ghosts" I was indoctrinated with and in which we are bombarded with every day-- anyone know what I mean?
My kids never cease to astonish and amaze me with their ability to learn and impact their world without curriculum and lessons.
I am looking forward to meeting some of you and gleaning wisdom from more experienced unschoolers. Hopefully I will help someone in the process.

I remember one aunt pointing out the toxicity of red or blue candies every time she saw the kids eating from them. Funny thing was she didnt mind second hand smoke in a closed up wintery room, or bug spray. I used to just smile and say " yeah" half-heartedly, mostly because we were guests at her home when the kids were given pocket money to go to the corner shop.
I suppose, in a way, maybe she thought she was being cool by not forbidding but reminding (nagging and funsucking). My kids LOVED hanging with their older cousins and the cats, dogs and chickens in the garden. It was worth it to sweetly ignore some annoying things...BTW none of the kids stopped eating the candies, nor have they become obsessed with them.

My computer desk is on the second floor of my house, and I have a window next
to my chair, which overlooks my neighbors roof. When I look out in the last
couple of weeks I am often seeing a bunch of baby squirrels playing and
cuddling and running around on the roof. It is absolutely adorable. There are three
right now, and they are alternating between playing chase and nuzzling each
other.

A few feet away from that, in an eave, there is a nest of sparrows. Given the
recent activities of the male and female, I expect there will be eggs and
babies soon.

This is so cool...just thought I'd share.

Kathryn

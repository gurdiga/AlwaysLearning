Hi all.. I've been a part of this list and the Unschooler one for several
months and I feel like I know many of you personally..I'm a lurker.. but a
poster-wanna-be! Maybe I'll have a chance of keeping up with this list..the
unschooling one moves too fast! I can't seem to stay on top of it all..by
the time I am ready to post, it's already been said and so much better! But
my family and hubby, hear your names so often..things will come up in
conversation at times and it will be something that has just been discussed
on this list too.
I have 3 girls.. Emily is 18 and mostly lives away from home now, Sarah 13
and Laura will be 8 next month. My hubby Russ and I have been married for 22
years..I have the coolest kids and hubby in the world! We live in the
country in SE Missouri, I raise Nubian milk goats, (I'm big into cheese
making) also Appaloosa horses, also raise chickens, ducks, and we have 4
dogs and 3 cats. I love to garden..veggies, herbs and lots of flowers
too..and the girls and I go on trail rides every weekend.. We started out as
school-at-home..but have been total unschoolers for at least the past 5
years. (Sandra.. you have been such a help and a great influence.. just want
to say 'thank ya'..)
I wish I could have you all over for an afternoon!
Julie in MO

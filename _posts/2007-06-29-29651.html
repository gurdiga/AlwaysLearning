kids have all misspelled words in the past week (as have I, and I
used a whole wrong word where I can't change it). So this isn't an
attack on this individual at all.

Here's what this is:
If you worry that your kids won't learn anything without school,
remember that some people DO go to school, for 12 or 13 years, and
still haven't become those ideally educated people that exist in our
imaginations. Those who spell well at the age of 20 probably
already spelled pretty well when they were twelve or thirteen. And
those who are musicians were probably already singing when they were
toddlers, and so forth. Very much of what people learn they learn
outside of and away from school.

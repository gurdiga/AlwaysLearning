We have two of these for our boys. They are light and
easy to move, which we do frequently. They are easy
to clean because the back removes from the seat and
the cloth cover removes to wash. They each have two
pull out cupholders which are also great for little
snacks and small toys. The arm rests are a good
length and the "wings" work well for when they fall
asleep in the car. The boys call them their "race car
seats" and think they are pretty cool.

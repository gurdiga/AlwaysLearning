Dear unschoolers,

I have subscribe to this list and the first post/digest were
numbered 25. Today I got the number 107 digest. Many posts were
exchanged but I have not had the opportunity to read most of
them. I have left the Unschooling.com mailing list to join this
one.

We have been unschooling our 7 yo in a neiborhood were we seem to
be the only homeschooler family. HSing is not yet a choice to
many in Japan. But we have been very busy and extremely happy
with our choice.

Next week, daughter and I are going to Sao Paulo, Brazil, where
my mother and my sister are and where I was born. We will come
back to Tokyo, the birth place of husband and his family, in the
beginning of April to resume our family/uschooling life.

I will unsubscribe fm this list tomorrow and subscribe again next
April. In Brazil I want to do things that I can not do here.
(like enjoying my family, the summer, the self-sufficient life -
eating what we grow- ..etc) Thank you very much for letting me
share your experiences and I promisse that this year I will share
our experiences too.

Bye!

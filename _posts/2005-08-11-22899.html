Hey,
This kind of late, I'm checking old emails and thought of an option to your question about
trying out new video games. My son tries out video games at gamefly.com. It works like
Netflix where you set up a wish list on the internet and they send you video games which you
can keep as long as you want. When you are tired of the game just send it back and they
send you another game from your wish list. I find that it saves us a lot of money because my
son isn't buying lots of games that later turn out to be boring or too easy for him. Then, if
he really likes a game he can buy it.

There's a brief article in the Sept. issue of Readers Digest about David
Sibley.
Although he is not a homeschooler or and unschooler, it is a good story
about a life spent pursuing what one loves, and how a real passion can't
be ignored.
There is a too small bit about how he learned the songs of birds just by
reading a description and seeing a chart, quite amazing.
You can get a feel from this article about the times in his life that
were inspiring and important and not one of them involved school.
David Sibley wrote the very good birding books, The Sibley Guide to Birds
and The Sibley Guide to Bird Life and Behavior.

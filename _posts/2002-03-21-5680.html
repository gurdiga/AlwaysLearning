The same way a conversation flows from one topic to another, unschooling life
can flow. One movie leads to another. An article in Smithsonian magazine
leads to the library. A song on the radio leads to getting the album out,
and showing the kids another version. That leads to getting on the internet
to see who wrote the song and who else has recorded it, and maybe printing
out the lyrics. That leads to playing it on an instrument. That leads to
cleaning a room.

Conversations don't happen if nobody talks.

Activities don't flow if there are no activities.

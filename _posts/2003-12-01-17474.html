Subject: [UUHomeschoolers] Reminder:December 1st Call In Day


Hello Everyone,

I am here to post this reminder about the Call in Day to Washington
DC about the homeschooling bill (HONDA) that is being discussed in
congress. I also wanted to tell everyone that there is a site that
is conducting an online petition about
the bill. Last night it had more than 750 signatures. You can add
your name* by going to
http://www.thePetitionSite.com/takeaction/807813448

*Signers may choose to hide their identity to the public. Such
names will appear as "Anonymous" on the PetitionSite.com site.
I hope you all will take the time to become informed about this very
important issue.

Here is the Call information-Please feel free to pass it along.

I would like to request that all homeschool groups and individuals
in this country that are not represented by HSLDA call the White
House on December 1 to let them know that :

A) All homeschoolers in this country are not represented by HSLDA
and Mike Farris
B) If you oppose HR2732/SB1562 please tell the White House/President
Bush
C) Remind them that federal legislation of education is
unconstitutional

Thanks.

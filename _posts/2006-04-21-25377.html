April Morris at 2006-04-21 21:45:37 +0300 said:

I completely agree! And I have been in the unfortunate position of following
through with my own mother. She refused to respect our choices. We were not
looking for approval, but demanded and expected respect. The same respect we
were willing to give to her choices. It was actually her partner that was
causing all the ruckus (I have never met a more condescending, rude,
disrespectful person in my life), but she had to make choices. We had about
2 years of not seeing her and very limited contact via phone. It was not
easy, but it was necessary and good. We actually have a very good
relationship with her now. She moved out on her own. She now respects our
choices and even agrees with some of what we do...what she doesn't agree
with she keeps to herself when the kids are around. We have a pretty good
time now when we get together.

I cannot imagine allowing anyone to say such horrible things to me or my
children. No one has that right, absolutely no one.

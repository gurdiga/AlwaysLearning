We scrub our vinyl pool weekly, we use it daily. Happiness to me is seeing Tim
dunk in after cycling home from work. He cycled yesterday. It was 107 in
Davis. We make a date and the two of us tackle the grime together, getting
filthy and wet and we too like the clean look of fresh water afterwards.I'll
have to hint to the kids how yummy homemade frozen yogurt would taste. Our
machine is modern and is a joyful experience whenever we make a batch of ice
cream. I bought our machine for my birthday and picked fresh strawberries too.


Our air cond. cut out yesterday. We will have it serviced tomorrow. It's
staying cool with the windows closed.

They do make chlorine tablets and ph kits for vinyl pools; I found ours at
WalMart. It's the same stuff as the bigger sizes for regular pools, though,
and I ended up buying the regular. I would put it in and stir with a long
net (we had one that was eight feet across) and it kept the changes down to
a minimum. It was a pain to clean!

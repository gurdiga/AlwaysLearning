I HATE chiggers. We are just north of San Antonio and they are terrible.
My 3 little guys are so ate up with them that I've made them stay inside for
the last few days. Michelle has lots of allergies and apparently chiggers
are no exception. Poor thing is covered with oozing welts. At least,
calamine lotion offers some relief. Adriane (11) decided to grow out her
fingernails and of course wouldn't quit scratching her chiggers. Ended up
with a staph infection that got into her eye and anywhere elastic was in her
clothes. Some Rx meds fixed her right up but it was very uncomfortable for
her...but at least she decided to cut her fingernails (doing the happy mom
thing because I miss her little girl fingers <sigh>)

Give me scorpions anyday. At least you have a chance of seeing them and
avoiding them.

I teach a couple of karate classes in my garage. (up to 14 students now
with 2 on a waiting list!!) One week we were invaded with worm snakes. Last
week, we had to pause sparring matches 3 times to kill a scorpion running
across the ring. I keep telling my students this is secret ninja
training.....but I don't think they are buying it <grin>

Julie

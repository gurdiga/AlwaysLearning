Hi All just had to share an aha moment of unschooling. I was baking
chocolate chip cookies (yes from scratch) while Max is napping and Steve and
Zak are building something with a Marbleworks "thing"... They are showing me
how they have set the structure up to give them more challenge in getting
the ball to go down... I was awestruck to stand there and watch my 6 year
old son and my 30something hubby playing together... I had to write and
share....

Just as I'm going to write my signature reality hits and Max wakes up
screaming from his nap and looking for mummy.....as I'm trying to finish
this off hubby is not sitting next to me comforting Max and Zak is laying on
the bed playing a car race game my mother gave him....

ahhh the joys of having a home business and unschooling.

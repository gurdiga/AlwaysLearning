Hi Katherine,

I'm happy to see you take an interest in your niece's condition. I
have MS and I often wish that my closest and dearest paid more
attention at times. A great place to start would be to join the
friendswithMS group on yahoogroups. Just send a blank email to
friendswithMS-subscribe@yahoogroups.com . They are a great bunch of
people and have a wealth of information. They'd be the ideal people
to get you the information you need.

GOod luck!

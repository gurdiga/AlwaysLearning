<<a sense of connectedness with all beings and things>>

I stumbled across a book called "Herbal Rituals". This book is written
month by month. The author talks almost poetically about the rhythms of
nature, noticing them, very intimate moments in her life with nature and
sharing her love of the earth with children. She also mentions how several
religions interpret the changes of nature, from Paganism to Judaism. In
each month/chapter, she talks about a particular herb and gives recipes for
various herbal mixtures. I thought it was a wonderful book and gave me a
renewed way to look at our earth and how to share that with my kids. We
made several of the herbal mixtures and continue to use them.

Julie--who is so very glad to be here

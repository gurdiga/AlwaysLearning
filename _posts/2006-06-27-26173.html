Entertainment Weekly which arrived yesterday announced an August 15
DVD release of The Weird Al Show. Anyone who doesn't know about
Weird Al should just probably buy the CD Poodle Hat and that will be
a good test of whether you'd want more. For those who have Poodle
Hat and all the other music, and the movie, might care about this.

What's cool is that the wrote a parody about Entertainment Weekly
asking him to write a parody to go with the announcement. It's
called "I will Comply" and is to the tune of "I will Survive." You
have to sing it yourself; he didn't record it. <g>
